Our Story

Built for One Metric: Your Return

Vora was built in New York by performance marketers who were tired of agencies that talked about brand awareness and sent vanity metrics in slide decks. We launched to do one thing: make every dollar you spend in ads return more than it cost.

From day one, we've been ROAS-first. Every campaign, every creative decision, every bid strategy is evaluated against a single question: does this generate measurable revenue for our clients?

Today we manage $100M+ in annual ad spend across Google, Meta, and programmatic channels for 300+ brands — from DTC startups scaling past $10M ARR to enterprise brands defending market share in competitive categories.

We're based in New York, obsessed with data, and allergic to excuses.

Common Questions

About Vora — Answered

Who founded Vora and when?
Vora was founded in 2020 in New York City with a singular focus: accountable, ROAS-driven performance marketing. The agency was built by practitioners who were frustrated with the industry's obsession with vanity metrics. From day one, every engagement has been structured around measurable revenue outcomes.
What makes Vora different from other performance marketing agencies?
Three things: ROAS accountability built into every retainer structure, full transparency (real-time dashboards, not monthly PDF reports), and a no-silo approach where Google Ads, Meta, and analytics teams work as one unit. Most agencies optimise channels in isolation — we optimise the full revenue funnel.
What industries does Vora specialise in?
Vora has deep experience in DTC e-commerce, SaaS, fintech, fashion, hospitality, and professional services. We've managed campaigns across competitive New York verticals and scaled brands from $1M to $50M+ in revenue. Our frameworks adapt to any industry with a measurable conversion event.
How large is the Vora team?
Vora operates as a focused senior team rather than a large agency with junior staff. Every client works directly with certified specialists — no account managers acting as intermediaries between you and the people doing the actual work. Our team includes Google Premier Partner-certified practitioners and Meta Blueprint alumni.
Does Vora work with businesses outside New York?
Yes — while Vora is headquartered in New York and has particular depth in NYC market dynamics, we serve clients nationwide. We currently manage campaigns for brands in Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, Austin, San Francisco, and 20+ other US cities. Digital advertising has no geographic boundary for performance delivery.
