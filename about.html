Our Story

Built for One Metric: Your Return

Vora was built in New York by performance marketers who were tired of agencies that talked about brand awareness and sent vanity metrics in slide decks. We launched to do one thing: make every dollar you spend in ads return more than it cost.

From day one, we've been ROAS-first. Every campaign, every creative decision, every bid strategy is evaluated against a single question: does this generate measurable revenue for our clients?

Today we manage $100M+ in annual ad spend across Google, Meta, and programmatic channels for 300+ brands — from DTC startups scaling past $10M ARR to enterprise brands defending market share in competitive categories.

We're based in New York, obsessed with data, and allergic to excuses.

What We Stand For

Four Principles. No Exceptions.

📈
ROAS-Obsessed

Return on ad spend is the only metric that matters. Every decision we make — from bid strategy to creative testing — is measured by what it returns. Brand vanity metrics don't pay your payroll. ROAS does.

Data-First

We don't run on gut feelings. We run on signal. Every campaign starts with audience intelligence, every optimization is hypothesis-driven, and every recommendation is backed by statistical evidence before it becomes a strategy.

Transparent

You see everything. Real-time dashboards. Weekly performance reports. Monthly strategy calls. No black boxes. No agency smoke-and-mirrors. If a campaign underperforms, we tell you why and show you exactly how we're fixing it.

Results-Only

We don't celebrate activity — we celebrate outcomes. Impressions don't count. Clicks don't count. Revenue counts. We structure our reporting and our retainers to ensure we're always accountable to what actually moves your business forward.
