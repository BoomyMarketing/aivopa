FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

What is included in the Starter plan?
The Starter plan at $1,500/mo includes single-channel paid media management — either Google Ads or Meta Ads. It covers campaign setup, ongoing bid optimization, conversion tracking, weekly performance reports, and a dedicated account strategist. We recommend a minimum monthly ad spend of $5,000 to generate sufficient data for meaningful optimization.
Is there a setup or onboarding fee?
There is a one-time onboarding fee of $750 for Starter plans. Growth and Scale plans include full onboarding at no additional cost. Onboarding covers account audits, pixel and conversion tracking setup, creative strategy, campaign architecture planning, and initial keyword or audience research.
Do you charge a percentage of ad spend?
No. All Vora plans are flat monthly retainers. Percentage-based pricing incentivizes agencies to increase ad budgets regardless of efficiency — we believe that's a misaligned model. Our flat fees keep our incentives tied to your ROAS, not your spend volume. For enterprise clients managing over $100K/mo, we offer a hybrid model on request.
What ROAS should I realistically expect?
Our portfolio average is 4.2x ROAS across all clients. eCommerce brands in fashion, beauty, and home typically achieve 3.5–6x ROAS within 90 days. Lead generation clients typically see 40–60% reductions in cost-per-acquisition (CPA). Individual results depend on industry, product margins, funnel maturity, and ad spend level. We'll give you a realistic ROAS forecast during your discovery call.
Can I cancel or change plans mid-engagement?
All plans require a 3-month minimum commitment. This is non-negotiable — paid media campaigns need 60–90 days of data to fully optimize machine learning signals and bid strategies. After the minimum term, plans roll month-to-month. You can upgrade at any time. Downgrades or cancellations require 30 days written notice.
