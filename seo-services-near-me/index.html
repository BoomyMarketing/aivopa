How Does Local SEO Differ from National SEO?

Local SEO focuses on proximity signals — Google Business Profile completeness, local citations, review velocity, and location-specific on-page content — whereas national SEO competes on domain authority, content depth, and backlink profiles. For local businesses, the Google Map Pack (the three business listings with the map that appear above organic results) is often more valuable than blue-link organic rankings because it appears first and is dominated by proximity and review signals rather than domain authority.

Vora's local SEO playbook addresses both layers simultaneously: we optimize your GBP for maximum local pack visibility while building neighborhood-specific content pages that capture the organic blue-link traffic below the map. This two-layer approach consistently outperforms single-layer strategies by capturing more page-one real estate across multiple search intent types.

How Do You Evaluate SEO Agency Performance Near You?

Most local SEO agencies send monthly rank reports. Rank reports show where you appear in search results — they don't show whether those appearances are generating revenue.
