Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

TikTok Ads

Best For: DTC, Consumer Brands, 18–35 Demo

TikTok's native-feeling creative format demands a different approach—and delivers outsized results for brands willing to invest in platform-native content. We manage Spark Ads, TopView, and In-Feed campaigns with continuous creative iteration to stay ahead of ad fatigue.

LinkedIn Ads

Best For: B2B SaaS, Enterprise, Professional Services

LinkedIn's unmatched B2B targeting—by job title, seniority, company size, and industry—makes it the highest-quality lead gen platform for B2B brands. We manage Sponsored Content, Message Ads, Lead Gen Forms, and Conversation Ads to drive qualified pipeline at target CPL.

Pinterest Ads

Best For: Home, Fashion, Food, Lifestyle, Wedding

Pinterest users are in discovery and planning mode—making it ideal for brands in verticals where visual inspiration drives purchase intent. We manage Shopping Ads, Promoted Pins, and Collections with audience targeting built on keyword, interest, and behavioral data.

Snapchat Ads

Best For: Gen Z, Mobile-First, DTC

Snapchat reaches a younger, mobile-native audience that's increasingly hard to reach on other platforms. We manage Story Ads, Collection Ads, and Dynamic Ads with creative optimized for full-screen vertical video and Snapchat's unique AR lens formats.

X (Twitter) Ads

X Ads deliver reach among high-intent, tech-forward audiences with interest and follower targeting. Best for brands building brand awareness alongside direct-response goals. We manage Promoted Posts, Amplify, and Lead Gen Cards across campaign objectives.

FAQ

Social Media Advertising Questions, Answered

Vora manages paid advertising on Meta (Facebook and Instagram), TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Snapchat, and X (Twitter). Platform selection is driven by your audience demographics, business model, and where your ICP actually spends time—not generic best practices. We typically recommend starting with 1–2 primary platforms and expanding as ROAS targets are hit.

We track ROAS using a combination of platform-reported attribution, Conversion API (CAPI) for server-side tracking, UTM-parameterized analytics, and where possible, multi-touch attribution models. For eCommerce we measure purchase ROAS. For lead gen we track cost per qualified lead (CPL) and closed-won attribution. We're transparent about the difference between platform-attributed and independently verified ROAS.

Vora produces static images, carousels, and short-form video creative for paid social—including direct-response UGC-style video, product demonstration videos, and testimonial edits. All creative is built to platform specifications, tested against performance benchmarks, and iterated on a 2-week creative refresh cycle to prevent ad fatigue and maintain strong click-through rates.

We implement Meta Conversion API (CAPI) for server-side event matching, which recovers a significant portion of attribution lost to iOS 14+ privacy changes. We also configure Aggregated Event Measurement (AEM) and prioritize conversion events appropriately. Additionally, we triangulate platform data with GA4, Northbeam, or Triple Whale where applicable to give you a holistic view of true paid social contribution.

Brands spending $5,000+ per month on paid social typically see clear ROI from professional management—through improved ROAS, faster creative iteration, and better audience strategy than a generalist in-house team. At $15,000–$30,000+ per month the complexity of creative testing, audience exclusions, bid strategies, and cross-platform attribution makes dedicated expertise essential for maintaining profitability at scale.
