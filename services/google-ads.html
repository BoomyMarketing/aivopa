Our Methodology

How We Achieve 4.2x ROAS Consistently

Most Google Ads accounts we inherit are bleeding 25–40% of their budget on non-converting queries, have poorly structured campaign hierarchies, and use broad match without proper negative keyword coverage. We fix all of that before we touch bid strategies.

01

Full Account Audit & Conversion Tracking Verification

We begin every engagement with a granular audit: conversion action health check, attribution model review, script analysis, audience list coverage, and quality score assessment. If your conversion tracking is broken or inflated, your bidding algorithms are optimizing toward ghosts. We fix tracking before anything else — a step most agencies skip.

02

Campaign Architecture Rebuild

We restructure campaigns around ROAS zones — grouping products or services by margin, competition level, and conversion velocity. Search campaigns use a tiered match type structure with exact match for high-intent, phrase for discovery, and broad with tight negative control for expansion. Shopping campaigns are structured with brand/non-brand splits and ROAS-target segmentation.

03

Negative Keyword Domination

We build negative keyword lists from your search term reports, industry exclusion lists, and competitor brand terms. Most accounts have fewer than 50 negatives — our accounts launch with 300–500 negatives and grow from there. This alone recovers 15–30% of wasted spend in the first 30 days, which we reinvest into high-converting ad groups.

04

Ad Copy & Creative Testing Engine

Responsive Search Ads with 15 headline variants, structured around benefit-first messaging, urgency signals, and ROAS-correlated CTA testing. We run ongoing RSA rotation analysis to identify which headline combinations drive the highest conversion rate — not just CTR. Display and YouTube assets are tested against CVR benchmarks, not viewability metrics.

05

Smart Bidding Optimization

We use Target ROAS and Target CPA smart bidding — but only once campaigns have sufficient conversion data (minimum 50 conversions per campaign over 30 days). Before that threshold, we use Manual CPC with aggressive segmentation to build the data foundation that smart bidding needs. We never rush into automated bidding before the algorithm has enough signal to perform.

06

Weekly Optimization & Monthly Strategy Reviews

Every week: search term pruning, bid adjustments, Quality Score monitoring, landing page performance review, and budget reallocation across campaigns based on ROAS trends. Every month: full strategic review — channel expansion opportunities, audience refinement, creative refresh cycles, and 90-day ROAS forecasting updated with actual performance data.

FAQ

Google Ads Management Questions Answered

What Google Ads campaign types do you manage?
We manage Search, Shopping, Performance Max, Display, YouTube (TrueView and Demand Gen), and Remarketing campaigns. Campaign mix is tailored to your product type, funnel stage, average order value, and ROAS targets. We audit your current setup and account history before recommending a campaign architecture — we don't apply templates.
What ROAS can I realistically expect from Google Ads?
Our portfolio average is 4.2x ROAS. eCommerce clients in high-margin categories (beauty, apparel, home goods) often achieve 5–8x ROAS within 90 days. Lead gen clients see 40–60% reductions in CPL within the same period. Individual results depend on industry competition, landing page quality, product margins, and starting account health. We always provide a realistic ROAS forecast based on a pre-engagement audit — not generic industry averages.
How do you reduce wasted ad spend?
We use aggressive negative keyword management (launching with 300–500 negatives per account), search term report analysis run weekly, match type discipline (no pure broad match without negative coverage), audience exclusions, and device/location/time-of-day bid adjustments. Most inherited accounts have 25–40% wasted spend — we typically recover that budget within the first 30 days and reinvest it into high-ROAS ad groups.
Do you manage Performance Max campaigns?
Yes. We have deep PMax experience and know how to guide its AI-driven targeting through strategic audience signals, well-structured asset groups with creative diversity, and conversion value rules that prioritize high-margin products. We actively monitor search term insights, placement exclusions, and conversion lag windows to ensure ROAS targets are maintained — not just accepted as PMax outputs.
What is your minimum ad spend for Google Ads management?
We recommend a minimum of $5,000/mo in Google Ads spend to generate statistically significant conversion data for meaningful optimization. Smart bidding algorithms need at least 50 conversions per campaign over 30 days to perform well. Our management starts at $1,500/mo (Starter plan). For budgets under $5K/mo, results are achievable but optimization timelines extend as data accumulates more slowly.
