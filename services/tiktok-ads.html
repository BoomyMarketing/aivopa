Ad Formats

Every TikTok Ad Format, Optimized for Revenue

TikTok is not a single-format platform. Each ad type serves a different funnel stage, audience signal, and creative requirement. We architect campaigns that combine formats intelligently — matching message to moment.

📱

In-Feed Ads

Native video ads in the For You feed — the core format for driving purchases. We build hook-optimized creative that stops the scroll in the first 2 seconds, holds attention through the offer, and converts with a friction-free CTA.

Best for: DTC eCommerce, impulse-purchase products under $100
✨

Spark Ads

Amplify existing organic posts — from your account or creator partners — as paid ads. Spark Ads inherit the social proof (likes, comments, shares) of the original post, creating the highest-trust ad format on TikTok with typically 30–40% better CVR than standard In-Feed.

Best for: High-engagement organic content, UGC with proven hooks
🛍️

TikTok Shop Ads

Direct-to-purchase shopping ads tied to your TikTok Shop catalog. Users purchase without leaving the app — reducing checkout friction dramatically. We manage catalog setup, product page optimization, affiliate creator coordination, and Shop ad bidding.

Best for: Beauty, health, kitchen, lifestyle products under $80
🎬

Video Shopping Ads

Shoppable in-feed videos with product anchors that let viewers tap directly to purchase. Combined with TikTok's product recommendation algorithm, VSAs reach high-intent buyers in the feed and on the Shop tab simultaneously.

Best for: eCommerce brands with TikTok Shop enabled
👑

TopView & Brand Takeover

Premium placements that appear when users first open TikTok. Guaranteed impressions and maximum reach — ideal for product launches, seasonal campaigns, and brand awareness pushes where CPM efficiency matters less than exposure volume.

Best for: Product launches, seasonal campaigns, brand awareness
🎯

Retargeting Campaigns

Re-engage users who've visited your website, added to cart, or engaged with your TikTok content. We build multi-touch retargeting sequences using Custom Audiences from Pixel events, email lists, and in-app engagement signals.

Best for: Cart abandonment recovery, cross-sell, win-back sequences

Our Methodology

The Creative Testing Engine Behind Our Results

On TikTok, creative is targeting. The algorithm distributes your ad based on how audiences engage with the content — which means the quality of your creative determines who sees it, not just the audience parameters you set. Our entire TikTok approach is built around this principle.

01

TikTok Pixel & Attribution Setup

We install and validate the TikTok Pixel with Events API server-side integration to capture purchase, add-to-cart, and initiate-checkout events with maximum accuracy. Most TikTok accounts we inherit have broken or under-reporting pixel setups — fixing attribution is the first step before spending a dollar on ads. We also configure UTM parameters and cross-reference TikTok data against Shopify or your analytics platform.

02

Competitor & Creative Intelligence Audit

We analyze your top competitors in TikTok's Creative Center and Ad Library to identify: which ad formats are driving the most engagement, what hooks are performing in your category, and where your creative positioning can differentiate. This research informs the first 4 weeks of creative testing and prevents you from spending budget rediscovering what's already known to work.

03

Hook & Format Testing (First 30 Days)

We launch 5 creative variants per week in the first month, testing: hook formats (text overlay vs. spoken hook vs. trending audio), video duration (7s vs. 15s vs. 30s), creator types (brand-direct vs. UGC creator), and offer framing (discount vs. benefit vs. social proof). Variables are isolated so winning elements can be compounded — not guessed. Our creative scoring system grades every ad on hook rate, watch time, CVR, and ROAS.

04

Scaling Winning Creatives

Once a creative achieves positive ROAS on a $50–100/day test budget, we scale spend methodically: 20% daily increases until CPM and CVR hold stable. We then build on the winning concept — new hooks with the same offer, new formats with the same hook — to extend creative lifespan. TikTok creatives fatigue faster than Meta; our structured rotation prevents the performance cliff that kills most TikTok campaigns after week 3.

05

Campaign Architecture & Bid Strategy

We structure TikTok campaigns with clear funnel separation: prospecting campaigns using Broad audiences and Lookalikes for acquisition, and retargeting campaigns using Custom Audiences from Pixel events. Budget allocation shifts weekly based on ROAS — more to what's working, less or off for what's not. We use Cost Cap bidding once conversion history exists, and Lowest Cost for new creative tests.

06

Weekly Reporting & Monthly Strategy Reviews

Every Monday: creative performance scorecard, spend pacing, CVR and ROAS trends, and next week's creative plan. Every month: full strategic review — channel ROAS vs. blended account ROAS, new audience opportunities, TikTok Shop performance if applicable, and 60-day creative roadmap. You see exactly what's running, why, and what we're testing next — no black-box reporting.

FAQ

TikTok Ads Questions Answered

How much does TikTok advertising cost in New York?
TikTok Ads management at Vora starts at $1,500/mo. We recommend a minimum TikTok ad spend of $3,000/mo to generate enough conversion data for meaningful optimization. TikTok CPMs typically run 30–50% lower than Meta, making it a highly cost-efficient channel for DTC brands willing to invest in creative production alongside media spend.
What makes TikTok Ads different from Meta Ads?
TikTok is a content-discovery platform. Users come to be entertained — which means your ads must feel native to the feed to avoid being scrolled past. Creative is the primary targeting lever: the right video reaches the right audience automatically through the algorithm. On Meta, audience targeting drives distribution; on TikTok, creative quality drives distribution. We build our entire TikTok approach around this insight.
Do you produce TikTok ad creatives?
Yes. Vora offers creative strategy, UGC briefing and creator coordination, script development, and creative performance analysis. We work with a vetted network of UGC creators to produce authentic content that performs in the TikTok feed. We run structured creative tests — 5 variants per week — to identify winning hooks, formats, and CTAs based on real conversion data, not gut feel.
What ROAS can I expect from TikTok Ads?
TikTok ROAS varies significantly by product category, creative quality, and price point. Beauty, fashion, and impulse-purchase products under $60 typically see 3–5x ROAS within 60 days. Higher-ticket products require more funnel engineering but benefit from TikTok's lower CPMs for awareness-to-consideration. We set ROAS targets based on your margins and CAC tolerance, then build a testing plan to hit them — not apply industry averages to your account.
Is TikTok Shop worth setting up?
For brands selling products under $100 in beauty, health, kitchen, or lifestyle categories, TikTok Shop is one of the highest-ROAS opportunities in US eCommerce right now. Commission rates, CPCs, and the native checkout experience combine to create economics that Meta and Google can't match for the right product. We manage the full setup: catalog integration, affiliate creator recruitment, product page optimization, and Shop ad management.
