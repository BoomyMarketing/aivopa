UGC Services

What's Included in UGC Ad Production

UGC production is more than finding a creator with a phone. Converting UGC requires the right creator match, a performance-optimized script, platform-specific editing, and systematic testing across hook variants and offer angles. We manage the entire UGC pipeline so you get ads that convert — not just content that looks authentic.

🎙️

Creator Sourcing & Matching

We match your brief to creators from our vetted network of 400+ UGC producers across beauty, health, fashion, food, tech, home goods, and finance verticals. Creator selection is based on vertical experience, on-camera authenticity, content quality, and brief-following track record — not follower count. We source 2–3 creators per concept so you can compare execution styles and select the strongest performer to scale.

Verticals: Beauty, Health, Fashion, Food, Tech, Home, Finance, Pets
📝

Performance Script & Brief Writing

Our creative strategists write UGC scripts engineered for conversion — not brand storytelling. Every script follows a proven structure: Hook (1–3 seconds, pattern interrupt or pain point), Problem (establish the relatable struggle), Solution (introduce your product naturally), Social Proof (specific result or transformation), Offer (clear CTA with urgency). Creators receive detailed briefs with scene-by-scene direction, approved talking points, must-include messaging, and visual reference examples. The brief is the product — strong briefs produce strong UGC.

3–5 hook variants tested per concept
✂️

Video Editing & Platform Optimization

Raw creator footage is edited in-house for maximum performance: dynamic captions (auto-synced, high-contrast for sound-off viewing), hook-first pacing edits (we often restructure the edit so the strongest moment leads), B-roll and product cut-ins, music licensing, logo placement per platform guidelines, and aspect ratio optimization (9:16 for TikTok and Reels, 4:5 for Meta feed, 16:9 for YouTube). Each final video is reviewed against platform-specific creative best practices before delivery.

Delivered in all required formats and aspect ratios
🧪

Creative Testing Framework

Every UGC concept enters a systematic test — hook vs. hook, offer angle vs. offer angle, creator A vs. creator B. We structure tests with isolated variables, sufficient spend per variant ($250 minimum before decisions), and time-boxed windows (7 days standard). Within 2 weeks we know which hook, creator style, and offer angle performs best for your audience. Learnings are documented as standing creative principles that improve every future brief — your UGC program gets smarter with every test cycle.

Decision rules: CTR, CPL, and ROAS benchmarks enforced weekly

TikTok Spark Ads

Spark Ads lets you boost existing TikTok posts from creator accounts — so your paid ad appears as native content from the creator's profile, not from a brand account. This dramatically improves authenticity signals (comments, shares, and engagement appear on the post) and TikTok's algorithm treats boosted organic posts more favorably than standard in-feed ads in auction. We handle creator authorization for Spark Ads as part of our UGC production — you get both organic and paid rights to every piece of content we produce.

Best format for TikTok — highest native appearance and engagement

Our Process

UGC Production Workflow

From brief to live campaign in 5–7 business days. Our production workflow is built for speed without sacrificing the strategic foundation that makes UGC convert rather than just look authentic.

01

Brand Onboarding & Creative Audit

We start by reviewing your current creative performance data: what's working, what's fatiguing, which audience segments need fresh creative most urgently. We analyze your existing UGC (if any), review competitor UGC strategies using TikTok Creative Center and Meta Ad Library, and identify the top 3 content angles — pain points, transformation stories, or social proof narratives — that are under-exploited in your category. This audit shapes the creative brief strategy for your first month of UGC production.

02

Creative Brief Development

For each content concept, we write a detailed creative brief: product talking points (approved messaging and specific claims), prohibited content (what creators must not say or show), target audience description, desired tone and energy (enthusiastic, informative, casual, authoritative), visual reference examples, hook options (3–5 variations the creator can choose from or we direct specifically), call-to-action requirements, and platform-specific format guidelines. Strong briefs take 2–3 hours to write — weak briefs produce generic content that doesn't convert regardless of creator quality.

03

Creator Matching & Contracting

We match each brief to 2–3 creators from our network based on vertical alignment, aesthetic match to your brand, on-camera communication style, and demonstrated brief-following ability. Contracts are issued digitally — covering content deliverables, revision rounds, usage rights, exclusivity period (creators won't produce content for direct competitors for 30 days), and payment terms. Product is shipped to creators within 24 hours of contract signing. For recurring programs, we develop preferred creator relationships with 3–5 creators per brand who understand your product and consistently deliver strong content.

04

Production & Review

Creators film and submit raw footage within 48–72 hours of receiving product. Our creative team reviews submissions against the brief: does the hook appear within the first 2 seconds? Are all required talking points covered? Is the framing and lighting acceptable for paid ad use? Is the CTA clearly delivered? We provide specific, actionable revision feedback when needed — not vague notes. Most creators deliver acceptable footage on the first submission; our vetting process filters out producers who can't reliably follow briefs before they're ever contracted.

05

Editing & Delivery

Approved raw footage goes to our editing team: hook restructuring (move the strongest moment to the opening 1–2 seconds), dynamic caption overlay, B-roll product cut-ins where available, background music (licensed through our subscription music library), color correction, and aspect ratio export in all required formats. We deliver final files in a shared Drive folder with platform-specific naming conventions. For TikTok Spark Ads, we also deliver the TikTok creator authorization workflow. All assets are delivered within 72 hours of approved raw footage — total brief-to-delivery is typically 5–7 business days.

06

Launch, Test & Iterate

We traffic UGC assets into your ad accounts and set up structured tests — each hook variant in a separate ad, isolated at the ad set level with equal budget allocation. After 7 days and a minimum spend threshold, we evaluate: CTR, hook retention rate (3-second video views as % of impressions), CPL/CPA, and ROAS. The winning hook and creator style become the creative brief template for the next production cycle. The losing variants are paused, and learnings are documented — we know why they lost, and we don't repeat the same mistake in the next brief.

FAQ

UGC Ads Questions Answered

What are UGC ads and why do they perform better?
UGC ads are video advertisements created to mimic authentic, organic social content rather than polished studio productions. They're filmed on smartphones by real-looking creators in a casual, first-person style that matches native content on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts. UGC ads outperform studio creative because they don't trigger the 'ad skip' reflex — they blend into the feed and hold attention long enough to deliver the message. On average, UGC-style ads achieve 4x higher CTR, 50% lower CPL, and 30% higher ROAS compared to polished studio creative on Meta and TikTok.
What types of brands work best with UGC ads?
UGC ads work best for consumer-facing brands where social proof and peer recommendation drive purchases. Top verticals: beauty and skincare (before/after testimonials), health and wellness (transformation stories), apparel and fashion (haul videos), food and beverage (taste tests), consumer electronics (unboxing and demos), home goods (real-home demos), and DTC subscriptions (unboxing reviews). B2B SaaS can also use UGC-style customer testimonials filmed in a documentary interview style for trust-building at higher price points.
What is the difference between UGC and influencer marketing?
UGC creators are paid to produce content assets that you own for paid ads — they're not posting to their own audiences. Influencer marketing pays for access to the influencer's followers. UGC creators are typically micro-creators or creators-for-hire valued for producing authentic-looking content, not for their reach. UGC is a creative production service; influencer marketing is a media buy. The two complement each other: you can amplify top influencer content with paid spend via whitelisting.
How do you source and vet UGC creators?
We source creators through our vetted network of 400+ UGC producers, platforms like Billo, Insense, and Minisocial, and direct TikTok and Instagram outreach. Vetting criteria: on-camera authenticity, content quality (lighting, audio, framing), demonstrated ability to follow briefs, vertical match, and reliability (turnaround time and revision responsiveness). We send product to 2–3 creators per concept and select the top-performing execution for paid deployment.
How many UGC videos do I need per month?
For active TikTok or Meta campaigns, we recommend 8–12 new UGC videos per month minimum — 2–3 new pieces per week. UGC content fatigue occurs faster than studio creative because audiences recognize the same 'authentic' content across multiple placements. High-spend accounts ($50k+/mo) may need 15–20 new pieces monthly. Each piece should test a different hook, creator type, or offer angle. Over time you build a library of winning UGC formats that compounds improvement with every test cycle.
