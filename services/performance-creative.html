Our Process

The Performance Creative Methodology

Creative that converts isn't made by feeling — it's made by running a structured testing program faster than your competitors. Our process turns creative production into a feedback loop that compounds improvement with every cycle.

01

Creative Audit & Competitive Analysis

We start by auditing your existing creative library — pulling performance data by format, message, and audience segment to identify what's working and why. We then run a competitive creative audit using Meta Ad Library and TikTok Creative Center to benchmark your creative against category leaders. This gives us a hypothesis stack for testing: which message angles are over-used in your category (avoid) and which are under-explored (opportunity).

02

Creative Strategy & Concept Development

We develop a monthly creative strategy document mapping out 4–6 concept directions per campaign: the pain-point hook, the social proof lead, the transformation story, the offer-first direct response, the educational/educational value play, and the authority/credential angle. Each concept has a defined hypothesis — which audience segment it targets, what objection it addresses, and what conversion metric we expect it to improve. Concepts are prioritized by testability, not creative preference.

03

Production & Asset Delivery

Production moves in weekly sprints. Week one: brief and concepting. Week two: production (filming, design, motion graphics). Week three: editing, revisions, platform spec optimization. Week four: trafficking and live tracking. Each asset is delivered in all required sizes and aspect ratios — we don't produce 9:16 and leave you to resize it. Static assets delivered in 1:1, 4:5, 1.91:1. Video in 9:16, 4:5, 16:9. All with captions and sound-off optimization.

04

Systematic A/B Testing

Each new concept enters a structured test: isolated variable testing (one change per test), sufficient budget allocation (minimum $250 per variant before decisions), and time-boxed evaluation windows (7 days standard, 14 days for low-volume accounts). We test one element at a time — hook vs. hook first, then offer vs. offer, then CTA format. This builds compounding knowledge about your audience instead of generating noise from multi-variable tests you can't decode.

05

Kill, Iterate, Scale Decisions

Every creative is evaluated against three hard thresholds every Monday: Kill (CTR below benchmark for 3 days, frequency above 4.0, CPA above target +30%), Iterate (CTR promising but conversion weak — test different landing page or offer), Scale (hits target CPA at statistical significance over $500 spend). Scaling winners means increasing budget on the ad set, duplicating to new audiences, and producing format variations (if video wins, make a static version; if 9:16 wins, make 4:5). No creative stays live on autopilot.

06

Monthly Creative Debrief & Next-Cycle Brief

Every month we deliver a Creative Performance Report: test results by concept, winning elements isolated, learnings documented as standing creative principles for your account. The debrief feeds directly into the next month's creative brief — so each cycle starts smarter than the last. Over 90 days, this compounds into a documented playbook of what works for your brand, your audience, and your offer — an asset that improves media efficiency for every campaign you run.

FAQ

Performance Creative Questions Answered

What is performance creative?
Performance creative is ad creative designed and evaluated primarily by conversion metrics — CTR, cost-per-click, ROAS, and CPA — rather than brand aesthetics. Every creative element (hook, headline, offer, visual, CTA) is treated as a variable to test systematically. Performance creative agencies produce many variants fast, kill losers within days, and scale winners — prioritizing learning velocity over creative perfection.
How many creative assets do I need per month?
For active Meta or TikTok campaigns, we recommend launching 3–5 new creative concepts per week minimum. Creative fatigue sets in within 7–14 days on high-spend accounts — once frequency rises above 3–4, CTR drops and CPM rises. Monthly, this means 12–20 new concepts, each tested in 2–3 format variations. Our performance creative packages deliver this volume with creative briefs, production, and in-platform trafficking included.
What makes a high-performing creative hook?
The hook is the first 1–3 seconds of video or the first visual of a static ad. A high-performing hook does one thing: stops the scroll. The strongest hooks use pattern interrupts (unexpected visual), direct pain-point statements, bold social proof claims, or native-feeling content that blends into the feed. We test 3–5 hooks per concept to find the scroll-stopper before scaling spend.
Do you produce video creative in-house?
We produce static, motion graphics, and edited video creative in-house. For native UGC-style video requiring on-camera creators, we source from our vetted creator network and direct content to match your brand voice and conversion goals. All production includes spec optimization for each platform (9:16 for TikTok/Reels, 4:5 for Meta feed, 16:9 for YouTube) and trafficking into your ad accounts.
How do you decide when to scale or kill a creative?
We use hard decision rules to remove emotion from creative evaluation. A creative is paused when CTR falls below platform benchmark for 3 consecutive days, frequency exceeds 4.0, or CPA rises more than 30% above target. A creative is scaled when it hits target CPA with 95%+ statistical confidence over $500 in spend and ROAS exceeds account target. Clear rules mean we act on data, not gut feel or creative attachment.
