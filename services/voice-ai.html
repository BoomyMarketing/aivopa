Our Process

How Vora Deploys Voice AI for Performance Marketers

We treat Voice AI as a performance channel—every decision is made to improve measurable outcomes like CAC, ROAS, and pipeline velocity.

1

Funnel Audit & Voice AI Mapping

We analyze your full funnel—ad platforms, CRM data, call recordings—to identify where voice automation creates the largest CAC and conversion rate impact.

2

Script & Logic Architecture

Our team builds dynamic conversation trees with branching logic, objection handling, and intent signals—trained on your actual prospect language and sales calls.

3

CRM & Ad Platform Integration

Voice outcomes sync to your CRM in real time. Call conversions import into Google Ads and Meta as offline events—improving Smart Bidding tCPA signals immediately.

4

A/B Testing & Voice Optimization

We run concurrent voice script tests—different hooks, CTAs, and objection flows—measuring qualification rate, booking rate, and revenue per call to continuously optimize performance.

5

Reporting & Attribution

Full attribution reporting connects every voice interaction to its originating ad click, campaign, and audience—giving you clear ROAS visibility across your voice AI investment.
