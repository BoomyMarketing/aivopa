Campaign Formats

YouTube Ad Formats We Master

YouTube advertising has evolved far beyond pre-roll awareness videos. Demand Gen, YouTube Shopping, and Shorts ads now deliver direct-response results comparable to paid search for the right products and audiences. We select and blend formats based on your funnel stage, budget, and conversion goal.

▶️

TrueView In-Stream

Skippable pre-roll and mid-roll ads — you only pay when users watch 30+ seconds or click. The most cost-efficient YouTube format for direct response. We engineer creative with a hook-driven first 5 seconds that retains viewers through to the CTA.

Avg. CPV: $0.05–$0.15 | Best for: High-consideration products, B2B, SaaS
🚀

Demand Gen

Google's newest performance format: AI-driven campaigns across YouTube In-Feed, YouTube Shorts, Gmail, and Discover. Functions like Meta prospecting — reaching new audiences with creative targeting rather than keyword or interest lists. Ideal for scaling beyond Search.

Best for: eCommerce brands wanting Meta-style prospecting via Google
🛍️

YouTube Shopping

Shoppable video ads with product carousels below the video. Users can browse and purchase directly without leaving YouTube. Requires Google Merchant Center integration and product feed optimization — we manage both alongside the campaigns themselves.

Best for: eCommerce with catalog of 10+ products, avg. order $30–$200
📱

YouTube Shorts Ads

Vertical video ads (up to 60s) between organic Shorts content. Massive reach among 18–34 audiences at CPMs often lower than TikTok. We produce and test Shorts creative using the same hook-testing framework as our TikTok programs.

Best for: DTC brands targeting 18–34 demo, impulse-purchase products
⏱️

Bumper Ads

Non-skippable 6-second ads, ideal for retargeting and brand reinforcement. We layer bumper campaigns on top of TrueView prospecting to maintain frequency with high-intent audiences who've viewed but haven't converted — driving them back through the funnel.

Best for: Retargeting, brand lift, frequency capping control
🎯

YouTube Remarketing

Re-engage users who've watched your videos, visited your channel, or viewed specific video types. Combined with website visitor lists from Google Ads, YouTube remarketing creates multi-touch sequences that dramatically reduce cost-per-conversion for warm audiences.

Typical ROAS: 5–10x on warm audiences with purchase intent signals

Our Methodology

How We Turn YouTube Views Into Revenue

Most YouTube campaigns underperform because agencies treat them as awareness channels with vague success metrics. We treat YouTube as a direct-response channel with hard ROAS targets — and the creative, targeting, and measurement strategy to prove it.

01

Conversion Tracking & Attribution Setup

We configure view-through conversion windows, cross-device tracking, and YouTube-specific conversion actions in Google Ads. Many advertisers misattribute YouTube's contribution because they only count click-through conversions — YouTube's impact primarily shows in view-through and assisted conversions. We set up proper attribution windows (typically 3-day view-through for direct response) so YouTube gets credited for what it actually drives.

02

Audience Architecture

We build layered audience targeting using: Custom Intent audiences (targeting users actively searching relevant keywords on Google), In-Market segments (users showing active purchase behavior), Customer Match lists (your email subscribers and customers), and YouTube channel engagement audiences (users who've watched your videos). Each audience layer targets a different funnel stage with appropriate creative and bidding.

03

Creative Scripting & Hook Engineering

The first 5 seconds determine whether a viewer stays or skips. We script TrueView ads with a disruptive visual hook (frame 1–2), a problem-identification statement (seconds 2–4), and a pattern interrupt that makes skipping feel costly. The remaining 25–55 seconds build the case with benefit-forward messaging, social proof, and a single clear CTA. We test 3–4 hook variants per campaign to identify the top performer within the first 2 weeks.

04

Placement & Exclusion Management

YouTube's placement targeting is powerful but requires active management. We build placement exclusion lists to prevent your ads from appearing on irrelevant channels, low-quality content, and brand-unsafe videos. We monitor placement performance weekly and move budget toward channel categories and specific placements generating the highest conversion rates — giving you better quality impressions at the same or lower CPM.

05

Full-Funnel Campaign Structure

We structure YouTube campaigns across three funnel stages: Prospecting (Demand Gen + TrueView targeting Custom Intent and In-Market), Mid-funnel (TrueView or Bumper ads targeting channel engagers and video viewers), and Retargeting (Bumper + Shopping ads targeting website visitors and cart abandoners). Budget is allocated by conversion velocity at each stage and rebalanced weekly based on ROAS performance.

06

Monthly Performance Reviews & Creative Refresh

YouTube creative fatigues within 4–8 weeks — we track view rates and CVR trends to identify fatigue signals before ROAS drops. Monthly reviews include: creative refresh scheduling, audience expansion opportunities, cross-channel ROAS comparison (YouTube vs. Meta vs. Google Search), and 90-day video production pipeline planning so you never run campaigns without fresh creative.

FAQ

YouTube Ads Questions Answered

How much does YouTube advertising management cost?
YouTube Ads management at Vora starts at $1,500/mo. We recommend a minimum YouTube ad spend of $3,000/mo for meaningful testing. YouTube CPCs for in-stream ads typically run $0.05–$0.30, making it one of the most cost-efficient video advertising channels for both awareness and direct-response campaigns at scale.
Is YouTube Ads good for direct-response or only brand awareness?
Both — and the distinction matters less in 2026. Demand Gen campaigns and YouTube Shopping ads deliver direct-purchase ROAS comparable to Google Search for eCommerce. TrueView In-Stream works exceptionally well for high-consideration purchases (software, finance, B2B) where the 30-second window allows problem-solution storytelling. We build YouTube strategies around your specific funnel goal, not a one-size approach.
Do you produce YouTube ad creative?
Yes. Vora provides creative strategy, script development, hook testing, and video performance analysis. We work with production partners for full video production when needed. At minimum, we deliver detailed creative briefs, shot-by-shot scripting, and hook/CTA testing frameworks so your existing video team or editors produce high-performing assets on the first attempt.
What is the minimum video length for YouTube Ads?
Skippable TrueView In-Stream ads must be at least 12 seconds long. Bumper ads are exactly 6 seconds. Shorts ads are vertical videos up to 60 seconds. For direct-response campaigns, we typically recommend 30–90 second skippable ads: strong hook in the first 5 seconds, problem-solution narrative from 5–25 seconds, and a clear CTA in the final 5 seconds. This structure retains viewers who would otherwise skip after 5 seconds.
How does YouTube advertising work with Google Search?
YouTube creates and captures demand — reaching people before they search by placing your message in their content feed. Google Search then captures the resulting intent. Together they form a powerful full-funnel system: YouTube introduces and convinces, Search captures the intent it helped generate. We manage both channels and provide cross-channel attribution reporting to prove the combined ROAS from the full system.
