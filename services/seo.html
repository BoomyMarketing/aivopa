SEO Questions, Answered

Most brands see measurable organic traffic growth in 3–6 months, with significant ranking improvements for mid-competition keywords by month 4–8. Highly competitive keywords in mature markets can take 9–18 months to reach page one. Technical fixes often yield faster wins—crawl errors resolved, Core Web Vitals improved, and canonical issues fixed can produce ranking lifts within weeks of implementation.

Vora's SEO service includes a full technical site audit, keyword research and content gap analysis, on-page optimization, monthly content production, internal linking strategy, schema markup implementation, Core Web Vitals optimization, and a proactive link building campaign. SEO reduces CAC by acquiring customers through organic search at near-zero marginal cost per click. As your site ranks for high-intent keywords, each organic visit costs nothing beyond the investment in SEO itself—compared to paid media where you pay for every click. As organic rankings compound over time, your blended CAC decreases because a growing share of conversions come from organic traffic rather than paid channels.
