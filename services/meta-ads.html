Meta Ads Management

Full-Funnel Meta Advertising That Scales Revenue

Vora's Meta Ads service covers every layer of performance—from account structure and creative to attribution, scaling strategy, and reporting.

🏗️

Account Structure & Campaign Architecture

Meta account architecture built for performance at scale—balancing Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns (ASC) for eCommerce ROAS, broad audience prospecting for new customer acquisition, and manual audience retargeting for high-intent converters. Structure adapts dynamically as account matures and budget scales.

🎨

Creative Strategy & Production

Creative is the primary performance lever on Meta. Vora's creative team produces direct-response static ads, carousels, UGC-style video, and Reels content built around proven hook-value-CTA frameworks. We refresh creative on 2-week cycles and test 3–5 new creative angles per month to stay ahead of ad fatigue.

📊

Creative Testing & Performance Analysis

Systematic A/B testing on creative hooks, formats, offers, and landing pages—with statistically rigorous analysis to identify genuine winners versus noise. Winning creative angles scale aggressively; underperformers are cut without sentiment. We report creative performance data weekly with clear next-action recommendations.

🔌

Conversion API (CAPI) & Attribution Setup

Server-side CAPI implementation to recover iOS 14+ attribution loss and improve event match quality scores. We configure pixel, CAPI, and Aggregated Event Measurement (AEM) in parallel—then verify attribution accuracy against GA4 and third-party tools. Better signal quality = better algorithmic optimization = lower CPA.

🎯

Audience Strategy & Scaling

Full-funnel audience architecture: broad prospecting to let Meta's algorithm find buyers, lookalike audiences built from LTV-optimized customer lists, and dynamic retargeting for catalog-based remarketing. We use audience exclusions aggressively to prevent budget waste and optimize frequency management at each spend level.

📈

Scaling Strategy & Budget Management

Disciplined scaling through incremental budget increases, campaign duplication testing, and marginal ROAS analysis that identifies exactly when to accelerate and when to hold. We maintain target ROAS through scaling by ensuring creative volume and audience depth expand in proportion to budget growth.

Campaign Types

Meta Campaign Formats Vora Runs for Performance

Each Meta campaign type serves a distinct role in the performance funnel. Vora selects and manages the right mix for your objective and budget.

Advantage+ Shopping (ASC)

Meta's AI-powered shopping campaign that finds buyers automatically. Highest-efficiency format for eCommerce brands with product catalogs and purchase conversion data.

Best for: eCommerce ROAS

Broad Audience Prospecting

Minimal audience restrictions letting Meta's algorithm find your best buyers at scale. Creative quality determines performance—not audience targeting refinement.

Best for: New Customer Acquisition

Dynamic Product Ads (DPA)

Catalog-fed retargeting showing product-specific ads to users who viewed or added to cart. Highest-intent retargeting format with proven purchase recovery rates.

Best for: Cart Recovery & Retargeting

Lead Generation Campaigns

Native Lead Gen Forms capturing qualified leads without leaving the app. Ideal for service businesses, B2B brands, and high-consideration purchases requiring lead nurturing.

Best for: B2B & High-Ticket Lead Gen

Awareness & Reach Campaigns

Upper-funnel awareness campaigns maximizing reach and frequency against target audiences—building brand familiarity that improves conversion rates across lower-funnel paid channels.

Best for: Brand Building & Retargeting Prep

Reels & Video Campaigns

Short-form video campaigns optimized for Reels and In-Stream placements. Lower CPMs with native-feeling creative that outperforms static in engagement and view-through attribution.

Best for: Awareness + DTC Video Creative

Creative Production

Creative Is the Variable That Wins on Meta

With broad audience targeting removing the audience-as-differentiator, creative quality is the primary performance lever on Meta. Vora treats creative as a performance function—not a brand exercise.

UGC-Style Video

Native-feeling creator-style video that blends organically into Meta feeds. Hook-value-CTA structure optimized for 3-second view rate, click-through, and conversion. Consistently outperforms polished brand video for direct-response objectives.

Direct-Response Static

High-contrast, message-first static ads with benefit-driven copy and strong CTAs. Tested across square (1:1) and vertical (4:5) formats for Feed, Story, and Reels Overlay placements.

Carousel Ads

Multi-card product showcases, feature-benefit breakdowns, and narrative-format carousels. Effective for eCommerce product discovery and SaaS feature education.

Testimonial & Social Proof Ads

Review and testimonial-led creative that leverages social proof to accelerate purchase decisions. Includes text-overlay review formats and talking-head testimonial edits.

FAQ

Meta Ads Questions, Answered

Vora structures Meta Ads accounts around proven frameworks for each objective: Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns (ASC) for eCommerce ROAS at scale, broad audience prospecting with creative-led targeting for brand building, and manual audience segmentation for specific ICP targeting in lead gen. We run a mix of Advantage+ and manual campaigns simultaneously—testing which performs best for each client's specific audience and offer—rather than dogmatically committing to one structure.

We implement Meta Conversion API (CAPI) for server-side event matching, which recovers a significant portion of attribution lost to iOS 14+ privacy changes. We also configure Aggregated Event Measurement (AEM) with appropriately prioritized conversion events. Beyond Meta's platform attribution, we triangulate with GA4, UTM tracking, and third-party MTA tools (Northbeam, Triple Whale) to give you a holistic, independently verified view of Meta's true contribution to revenue.

Vora produces the full creative suite for Meta performance: direct-response static images, carousels, UGC-style video, product demo videos, testimonial edits, and short-form Reels ads. All creative is built to placement specifications (Feed, Reels, Stories) and tested on a 2-week creative refresh cycle. We track performance by creative angle, format, and hook to continuously optimize our creative output toward the highest-ROAS variants.

Brands spending $5,000–$10,000/month on Meta can benefit from professional management—gaining creative testing velocity and audience strategy that in-house generalists rarely match. The ROI of expert management becomes clearly measurable at $15,000–$30,000/month, where systematic creative testing, bid strategy optimization, and attribution rigor produce significant ROAS improvements versus DIY management. Vora's Scale plan is designed for brands spending $30,000+ per month seeking maximum performance.

Scaling Meta spend while maintaining ROAS requires creative velocity, audience expansion, and bid discipline—all working together. We scale through creative diversification (more winning angles prevent fatigue), audience broadening (trusting Meta's algorithm with more budget to find converters), and incremental budget increases (10–20% weekly increases that give the algorithm time to reoptimize). We also identify scaling ceilings proactively using marginal ROAS analysis to know exactly when to push and when to hold.
