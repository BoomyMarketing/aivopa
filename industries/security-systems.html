How We Generate Security System Leads in New York

Security buyers research carefully before inviting an installer into their home or business. Every campaign is built around capturing high-intent searches, building trust through verification and reviews, and converting one-time installations into long-term monitoring contracts.

🔍
Google Search Ads
High-intent campaigns capturing "home security installation NYC," "alarm system installer Brooklyn," and "commercial security camera Manhattan" — homeowners and business owners who have made the decision to install security and are evaluating which company to hire. Campaigns structured by customer type (residential vs. commercial) and system type (cameras, alarms, access control) to match bids and messaging to the specific inquiry value.
✅
Local Services Ads (LSA)
Google Guaranteed badge at the top of security installation searches — verification is the most critical trust signal for an industry where technicians access private property and install equipment that protects the client's home or business. LSA verification, combined with strong review scores, consistently converts security inquiries at higher rates than non-verified competitors regardless of price positioning.
📍
Local SEO & Google Business Profile
Map-pack rankings for security system searches across NYC boroughs — GBP optimized with installation photos, product brands carried, licensing credentials, service area map, and review solicitation sequences. License and insurance information displayed prominently in the GBP profile addresses the primary trust objections that prevent security inquiries from converting to booked consultations.
🏢
Commercial Business Targeting
Retail stores, restaurants, offices, medical practices, and warehouses require commercial-grade security — installations worth $2K–50K+ with ongoing monitoring contracts. We build LinkedIn and Google Search campaigns targeting business owners and facilities managers with commercial system positioning, references from comparable NYC business types, and licensed commercial installer credentials that residential-focused competitors can't match.
🔄
Monitoring Contract Acquisition
Professional monitoring ($30–80/month residential, $100–500+/month commercial) is the highest-lifetime-value revenue in security. We build installation campaigns that present monitoring as the natural completion of a security system, and build retargeting sequences that convert installation clients who initially declined monitoring. A client on a $50/month monitoring plan generates $600/year with minimal additional service cost.
🏠
Smart Home Integration Campaigns
Smart home integration (Ring, Nest, smart locks, video doorbells) represents the fastest-growing segment of residential security in NYC — homeowners who start with a video doorbell become candidates for full alarm systems and professional monitoring. We build campaigns around smart home security entry points that capture the broader home automation buyer and present professional installation and monitoring as the premium upgrade over DIY alternatives.
