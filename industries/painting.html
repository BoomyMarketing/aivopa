FAQ

Painting Contractor Marketing Questions

Google Search and LSA (Local Services Ads / Google Guaranteed) are the two highest-ROI channels for painting contractors. Search captures residential queries like "interior painter NYC" or "apartment painting Manhattan." LSA puts you at the very top of results with the Google Guaranteed badge and charges per lead rather than per click — critical for a service where trust and credential verification matter. Local SEO and Google Business Profile drive organic lead flow for neighborhood-level searches. Meta ads work for retargeting and before/after portfolio content that generates word-of-mouth referrals.
Commercial painting accounts — office buildouts, retail renovations, co-op and condo hallways, property management contracts — are won through a combination of Google Search (targeting "commercial painting contractor NYC" queries), LinkedIn outreach to property managers and general contractors, and referral relationships with real estate brokers and building managers. Commercial jobs range from $5K–200K+ and often involve recurring contracts for multi-building portfolios. We build separate targeting and landing pages for commercial painting so residential and commercial campaigns don't cannibalize each other.
Spring (March–May) is the highest-demand season for interior residential painting in New York — homeowners and co-op owners emerge from winter wanting fresh interiors. Summer is strong for exterior painting when weather permits. Fall pre-holiday painting (September–November) is a secondary peak as clients refresh before Thanksgiving and Christmas gatherings. We pre-load seasonal campaigns 3–4 weeks before each peak, raise budgets as demand rises, and pull back efficiently during slower winter weeks — so you're never overpaying for slow-season clicks.
The two biggest differentiators in the NYC painting market are speed of estimate and portfolio quality. We build campaigns that lead with "same-week free estimate" and drive to a simple, fast estimate request form — reducing friction for time-pressed Manhattan apartment owners. Before/after photo content in ads and on landing pages is the most effective trust builder for first-time clients. Google reviews with specific details (job type, neighborhood, quality of finish) build the credibility that converts estimate requests into booked jobs.
Google Search CPCs for painting contractor terms in NYC range from $5–25. CPL typically runs $40–130 per estimate request. LSA pricing is pay-per-lead at $30–80 per lead. Most painting contractors start with $2K–6K/month in ad spend. With average residential jobs running $2K–15K and commercial jobs $10K–200K+, the return on qualified lead generation is strong. Vora's management fees are separate from ad spend and are tied to cost-per-estimate-request targets.
