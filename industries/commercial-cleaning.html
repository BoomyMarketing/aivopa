How We Generate Commercial Cleaning Contracts in New York

NYC is the largest commercial real estate market in the country — millions of square feet of office, retail, medical, and hospitality space require professional cleaning services. Every contract we generate is worth $24K–300K over its term.

🔍
Google Search Ads
High-intent commercial cleaning searches from facilities managers and office managers in active vendor evaluation mode — 'commercial cleaning company NYC,' 'office cleaning service Manhattan,' 'janitorial services Brooklyn,' 'commercial cleaning contract New York,' 'medical office cleaning NYC,' 'restaurant cleaning service Queens.' These searches represent buyers who are either replacing an existing vendor (a high-frustration, high-urgency decision trigger) or setting up services for a new facility (an immediate decision with no incumbent vendor). Campaign architecture segments by facility type (office buildings, medical practices, restaurants, retail, warehouses) because each facility type has different cleaning requirements, frequency schedules, and compliance standards — a medical office cleaning campaign emphasizes disinfection protocols and OSHA compliance, while a restaurant cleaning campaign emphasizes health department inspection readiness and after-hours scheduling. Commercial cleaning searches have lower CPCs than consumer service categories ($5–20 per click) but generate much higher value contract inquiries — a single qualified lead that converts into a contract generates $24,000–300,000 in total revenue.
💼
LinkedIn B2B Outreach
LinkedIn is the highest-quality proactive acquisition channel for commercial cleaning companies — allowing direct outreach to facilities managers, office managers, operations directors, and property managers at companies with 50+ employees in NYC office buildings before they're actively searching for a new vendor. Sales Navigator targeting identifies the exact decision-maker profiles at companies in target industries (law firms, financial services, tech companies, medical practices, co-working spaces) within specified Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens building addresses. Outreach sequences position the cleaning company's specific specialties — LEED-certified green cleaning products, COVID-enhanced disinfection protocols, 24/7 operations manager availability, digital quality-control inspection reports — as relevant to the target company's industry and building type. LinkedIn outreach programs generate 3–8 qualified site assessment conversations per month for well-executed campaigns, each representing a contract opportunity worth $12,000–60,000+ annually. The longer B2B sales cycle (30–90 days from first outreach to contract signing) means LinkedIn programs require sustained investment before yield materializes, but generate the highest-quality leads with the lowest long-term CPL of any channel.
🏗️
Property Manager Campaigns
Property management company partnerships are the highest-volume contract referral source for established NYC commercial cleaning companies — a single property management firm managing 20–50 commercial buildings can generate referrals for dozens of cleaning contracts worth hundreds of thousands of dollars annually. NYC's commercial real estate market is managed by a concentrated group of large and mid-size property management firms whose managers routinely receive tenant requests for cleaning vendor recommendations. We build property manager relationship programs: direct outreach to property managers at target firms offering preferred vendor status, response-time guarantees, and transparent pricing frameworks; portfolio content demonstrating the cleaning company's work in class-A office buildings with before/after documentation and supervisor references; ongoing relationship-maintenance programs (quarterly service reviews, case study content, proactive communication) that keep the cleaning company top-of-mind when a property manager needs to recommend a vendor. Companies with 3–5 active property management firm relationships generate 40–60% of new contract volume through referrals at near-zero acquisition cost versus $200–800 CPL through paid channels.
📅
New Office Opening Targeting
New office openings represent the highest-converting contract acquisition opportunity — companies moving into new space have no incumbent cleaning vendor, are making all facility service decisions simultaneously, and are in active evaluation mode during the move-in period. NYC processes thousands of commercial lease transactions annually, each a potential cleaning contract with no competitive displacement required. We build new office opening targeting programs: commercial real estate listing monitoring to identify recently signed leases and impending tenant move-ins; direct outreach to companies announcing new NYC office openings via LinkedIn and press release tracking; and partnerships with commercial moving companies, office furniture vendors, and interior designers who work with companies during move-in and can refer cleaning companies during the setup period. New office opening leads convert at 2–3x the rate of replacement vendor inquiries because there is no incumbent to displace — the cleaning company simply needs to provide a competitive proposal and reliable references during the short evaluation window before the company selects a service provider and signs a contract.
📍
Local SEO & Google Business Profile
Map-pack rankings for 'commercial cleaning near me,' 'office cleaning company [borough],' and facility-type-specific searches like 'janitorial services Manhattan' or 'commercial cleaning Brooklyn' — facilities managers often use location searches when evaluating vendors for buildings in specific neighborhoods. GBP optimization for commercial cleaning companies includes: service area configuration covering all five boroughs and the surrounding commercial districts; photo gallery documenting completed commercial cleaning work in diverse facility types (office spaces, medical offices, retail, restaurants); review generation programs targeting facilities managers and office managers at current accounts (B2B reviews carry significant weight in commercial vendor selection); and service listing with facility types, compliance certifications, and service frequency options. Commercial cleaning companies with 30+ Google reviews from business clients and 4.7+ ratings convert significantly more map-pack searchers into site assessment inquiries than companies with few or no B2B-specific reviews.
🤝
Account Referral & Expansion
Commercial cleaning account referrals and service expansion are the two highest-margin growth strategies for established cleaning companies — both generate revenue with near-zero acquisition cost compared to new-channel lead generation. Account referral programs structure the natural tendency of satisfied facilities managers to recommend vendors to peers in their building, their industry network, and neighboring companies: formal referral programs offering service credits or contract discounts for accounts that generate signed new-client referrals, and proactive referral request timing (30–60 days after a new account's first positive service cycle, when satisfaction is measurable and the manager has had enough experience to recommend confidently). Service expansion programs systematically identify opportunities to expand within existing accounts: current daily office cleaning accounts that might add quarterly deep-clean services, post-construction cleaning, window cleaning, floor care, or specialized disinfection services. Expanding an existing account's service scope generates 40–80% gross margin on incremental revenue with zero additional sales overhead — making service expansion the single highest-ROI growth activity for an established commercial cleaning company.

Built for NYC Commercial Cleaning Companies

Commercial cleaning marketing in New York requires B2B account acquisition strategy, property manager relationship programs, and long-cycle sales pipeline management — not the residential cleaning marketing playbook.

01
Contract-Based Revenue Model
Commercial cleaning is a contract business — most accounts sign 1–3 year agreements with monthly service fees and defined scope, making the total contract value ($24,000–300,000+) the relevant metric for evaluating marketing investment, not individual service ticket value. We build marketing programs calibrated to contract economics: CPL targets based on contract value and average margin rather than per-service revenue; lead qualification systems that prioritize facilities with 5,000+ sq ft of cleanable space (the minimum threshold for economically viable commercial accounts in NYC); and account scoring frameworks that identify which inbound inquiries represent the highest-value contract opportunities for priority follow-up. Commercial cleaning companies that track pipeline value in terms of total contract value (not monthly invoice value) consistently make better marketing investment decisions and achieve higher overall ROI from their customer acquisition programs.
02
B2B Sales Cycle Management
Commercial cleaning B2B sales cycles run 30–90 days from initial inquiry to signed contract — longer than consumer service categories but with dramatically higher contract values that justify the extended process. The typical commercial cleaning sales cycle involves: initial inquiry or outreach response, site assessment and facility walkthrough (often within 5–10 business days of initial contact), proposal submission and competitive bid comparison (2–4 weeks), facilities manager or operations director approval (sometimes requiring CFO or building owner sign-off for large accounts), and contract negotiation and signature. We build sales pipeline infrastructure for commercial cleaning companies: CRM-integrated lead tracking that maintains visibility on each prospect's position in the sales cycle, automated follow-up sequences for proposals submitted but not yet decided (typically the highest-value intervention point in the commercial cleaning sales process), and proposal templates calibrated to the specific concerns of each facility type (medical practices want compliance documentation, restaurants want health inspection readiness, law firms want confidentiality protocols). Companies with systematic sales pipeline management close 20–35% of qualified site assessments into signed contracts versus 8–15% for companies managing their pipeline informally.
03
NYC Commercial Real Estate Intelligence
NYC's commercial real estate market is geographically concentrated and building-type-specific in ways that create significant opportunities for commercial cleaning companies with market-specific knowledge. Midtown Manhattan (Park, Madison, Lexington, 5th Avenue corridors) concentrates the highest density of large law firm, financial services, and media company accounts with the largest cleaning budgets. Downtown Manhattan (Wall Street, FiDi, Tribeca) has significant financial services and co-working space concentration. Midtown South and Chelsea have become tech company and creative agency hubs with newer, higher-specification office buildings. Brooklyn (DUMBO, Downtown Brooklyn, Industry City) has grown into a significant commercial office market. We build geographic campaign targeting strategies that allocate budget proportionally to account opportunity density: higher Search bids and LinkedIn targeting coverage in Midtown Manhattan where the highest-value accounts concentrate, and market-expansion campaigns in Brooklyn and Queens where commercial cleaning competition is lower and contract win rates are higher. Companies with geographic targeting strategies consistently generate more qualified pipeline per marketing dollar than companies running uniform city-wide campaigns.
