FAQ

Accounting Firm Marketing Questions

Google Search is the highest-intent channel for accounting leads — business owners searching 'CPA firm NYC' or 'small business accountant New York' are ready to hire. Local SEO and Google Maps optimization capture searchers in your borough or neighborhood. LinkedIn is effective for B2B-focused practices targeting CFOs, controllers, and business owners at specific company sizes. Review generation on Google and Yelp is foundational — accounting is a trust-based purchase.

Tax season (January–April 15) is the highest-intent search period for accounting services. We scale Google Search budget significantly starting January 1, run offer-specific campaigns ('Free Tax Consultation,' 'S-Corp Election Deadline'), and pause or reduce spend post-April 15. We also plan Q3 campaigns targeting business owners thinking about year-end tax planning (September–November) and bookkeeping clients during Q1 new-year-planning intent.

Small and mid-size businesses (SMBs) with ongoing bookkeeping and tax needs are the highest-LTV accounting clients — a business owner who uses you for monthly bookkeeping, quarterly taxes, and annual returns can be worth $5,000–25,000/year for 5–10+ years. We prioritize campaigns that attract business owner clients over individual filers. Industry-specific targeting (real estate, healthcare, restaurants, e-commerce) often yields clients with complex needs and higher billings.

Critical. Accounting is one of the highest-trust service categories — business owners compare Google review counts and ratings before calling. Firms with 50+ 5-star reviews dramatically outconvert firms with fewer reviews, even when spending the same on ads. We build automated review request sequences triggered at the right moment (after tax return delivered, after year-end close) to systematically build review volume without being intrusive.

Google Search generates first leads within days of launch. Local SEO improvements take 60–90 days to show meaningful ranking gains in local pack results. LinkedIn outreach and content campaigns typically generate first responses within 2–4 weeks. For a new campaign, expect first qualified leads (business owner inquiries) within 2 weeks. Stable CPL benchmarks and consistent lead flow typically establish within 45–60 days as campaigns optimize.
