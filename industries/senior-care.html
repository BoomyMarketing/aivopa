Referral Source Development

Hospital discharge planners, social workers, geriatric care managers, and physicians are high-value non-digital referral sources. We develop outreach content, digital profiles on referral networks (Caring.com, A Place for Mom), and CRM workflows that systematize referral relationship building.

HIPAA-Compliant Tracking

Senior care marketing involves sensitive health information. We configure HIPAA-compliant tracking that avoids PHI transmission to ad platforms. Conversion tracking uses aggregate events only (form completion, call, consultation booked) — no individual health data shared with Google or Meta.

Our Process

How We Build Your Senior Care Client Pipeline in 30 Days

1
Service & Capacity Audit

Define your service types, geographic coverage, caregiver capacity, and payer mix (private pay, Medicaid, long-term care insurance). Identify which care types have the highest margin and capacity — these lead campaign targeting priorities.

2
Decision-Maker Audience Build

Build Google and Meta audience segments targeting adult children in caregiver-need indicators: age 45–65, proximity to elderly populations, past searches on senior care topics. Exclude irrelevant audiences to maximize budget efficiency.

3
Trust-Centered Creative & Landing Pages

Build landing pages leading with trust signals: caregiver certifications, years in operation, licensing, family testimonials, and BBB rating. Compassionate language, no fear-based messaging. Include a free consultation CTA with same-day callback promise.

4
Campaign Launch & HIPAA Tracking Setup

Launch Google Search for high-intent queries plus Meta for caregiver-need audiences simultaneously. Configure HIPAA-compliant call tracking and form attribution. Immediate follow-up automation (call + email within 15 minutes of inquiry) — speed-to-contact is critical in crisis-driven senior care searches.

5
Caregiver Recruitment Launch

Launch parallel caregiver recruitment campaigns targeting HHAs and CNAs in your service area. Build an applicant pipeline ahead of client demand so you're never turning away families due to staffing. Track caregiver hiring rate alongside client inquiry rate.

6
LTV & Referral Reporting

Monthly reporting on inquiries, consultations, care starts, and retention by channel. Track referral source volume (Google, Meta, referral partners, directories). Optimize budget toward channels producing the highest-LTV, longest-retention clients.
