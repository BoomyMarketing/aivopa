Meta & TikTok DTC

Pet UGC featuring real animals consistently outperforms studio creative. We test pet owner testimonials, before/after health results, and "day in the life" content across Meta and TikTok to drive DTC first purchases with trial offers.

Amazon Advertising & DSP

Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Amazon DSP for pet brands. We optimize for Subscribe & Save adoption, target breed and life-stage search terms, and retarget product page viewers with DSP to close the purchase loop.

Pet Influencer Campaigns

Micro and mid-tier petfluencers on Instagram and TikTok drive exceptional engagement and conversion. We source, brief, manage, and track pet influencer campaigns — and repurpose top content as paid dark posts on Meta.

Subscription & Retention Email/SMS

Subscription models are uniquely powerful in pet — auto-ship buyers are worth 5–10x one-time purchasers. We build Klaviyo flows that convert first buyers to subscribers: win-back, loyalty rewards, and churn-prevention sequences.

🏪
Retail Velocity Campaigns

For pet brands in Petco, PetSmart, or independent retail: we run geo-targeted ads, in-store QR promotions, and digital co-op campaigns that move product off shelves and build sell-through data for buyer negotiations.

LTV & Attribution Modeling

Pet brand success is a LTV game. We model subscription cohort LTV, CAC payback periods, and channel-level ROAS using Triple Whale or Northbeam. Weekly reporting shows true business health beyond last-click platform metrics.

Our Process

How We Grow Pet Brands in 60 Days

1
Brand & Category Audit

Analyze your current DTC, Amazon, and retail performance. Identify top-converting SKUs, subscription rate, AOV, and LTV benchmarks vs category leaders.

2
Creative Strategy & UGC Brief

Brief pet influencers and existing customers for authentic UGC. Develop ad creative angles: ingredient stories, before/after health outcomes, lifestyle content, and subscribe & save CTAs.

3
Multi-Channel Launch

Launch Meta acquisition campaigns, Amazon PPC, and influencer seeding simultaneously. Coordinate messaging and offers across all channels for maximum first-purchase efficiency.

4
Subscription Conversion

Activate Klaviyo post-purchase flows that move first-time buyers to subscriptions within 30 days. A/B test subscription incentives, delivery frequency, and bundle offers.

5
Scale & Retain

Double budget on proven ad sets and creative angles. Expand to TikTok Shop, Amazon DSP, and YouTube. Launch retention flows for churned subscribers with win-back offers.

6
LTV Optimization

Model cohort LTV by acquisition channel, creative type, and first SKU. Shift budget toward channels with the best LTV:CAC ratio — not just lowest CPL. Monthly strategy reviews with your team.

Pet owners are emotionally driven buyers with extremely high LTV — the average dog owner spends $1,900/year on their pet.
