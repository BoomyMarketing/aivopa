Our Process

How We Grow Law Firms in NYC

Legal marketing in New York requires keyword precision, budget discipline, and intake alignment — so every dollar spent maps to a qualified consultation, not a wasted call.

1

Practice Area & Competitor Audit

Map CPC benchmarks for your practice area in NYC, identify competitors' ad copy, and audit your current website for landing page quality. Legal CPCs vary 10x between practice areas — budget allocation depends on case value economics.

2

Keyword Architecture

Build tightly themed ad groups around each practice area and case type. Layer negative keywords for research queries, student/career traffic, and other practice areas. Legal has one of the highest negative keyword burdens in any category.

3

LSA Setup & Verification

Submit law firm credentials for Google Screened verification. Set service areas, practice areas, and budget cap. LSA requires attorney bar number, malpractice insurance, and background check — we manage the documentation process.

4

Landing Page Build

One landing page per practice area: clear headline matching search intent, intake form above the fold, free consultation CTA, social proof (Google reviews, Avvo rating, case results), and call number in the header. No generic firm homepage as the landing page.

5

Call Tracking & CRM Integration

Unique tracking numbers per campaign. CallRail for recording and transcription. Intake form leads pushed to Clio or Lawmatics. Monthly review of call recordings to identify unqualified lead patterns and refine negative keywords.

6

Monthly Optimization

Bid adjustments by device, time of day, and geographic sub-area (Manhattan vs. Brooklyn vs. Bronx). Creative refresh every 60 days. Review LSA lead quality and dispute invalid leads for credits. Quarterly practice area expansion review.

FAQ

Law Firm Marketing Questions

What practice areas do you work with?

Personal injury, family law (divorce, custody), criminal defense, immigration, estate planning, employment law, real estate law, bankruptcy, and business law. Each practice area has different CPCs, conversion rates, and intake quality — we tailor bidding strategy and landing pages to the specific economics of your practice.

How much do legal Google Ads cost in NYC?

Legal is one of the most competitive Google Ads categories. Personal injury CPCs in NYC range from $50–$200+ per click. Family law and criminal defense run $20–$80 per click. Local Services Ads (LSA) are often more cost-efficient — you pay per lead, not per click, and Google verifies the firm's credentials before showing the ads.

What are Local Services Ads and should my firm use them?

Local Services Ads (LSA) appear above regular Google Ads and include a 'Google Screened' or 'Google Guaranteed' badge. You pay per lead, not per click. For most practice areas in NYC, LSA generates the highest-quality leads at the most efficient cost — we recommend running LSA alongside traditional Search campaigns for maximum coverage.

How do you track lead quality for law firms?

We implement call tracking (CallRail) to record and transcribe inbound calls, form submission tracking with case type classification, and CRM integration (Clio or Lawmatics) to track which leads become retained clients. Optimizing for retained clients, not just form fills, is what separates profitable legal marketing from expensive lead generation.

How long before we see new case inquiries from paid search?

Google Ads can generate inbound calls and form submissions within 24–48 hours of launch. Volume ramps over 30–60 days as Quality Score improves and bidding algorithms learn from conversion data. LSA typically takes 2–4 weeks for verification and onboarding before leads begin flowing.
