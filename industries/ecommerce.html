Our Process

How We Grow Ecommerce Brands

A structured 60-day sprint — foundation in the first two weeks, creative testing through day 30, scaling profitable campaigns through day 60, and retention compounding from month one onward.

1

Tracking & Attribution Audit

Implement CAPI, verify GA4 purchase event accuracy, connect Klaviyo, and set up a blended ROAS dashboard. No campaign optimization without clean data — this is non-negotiable week one.

2

Audience & Competitive Research

Map your best-performing SKUs, highest-LTV customer segments, and the creative angles competitors are running. We find the differentiation angle before writing a single ad.

3

Creative Testing Sprint

Launch 5–8 new creative concepts in a dedicated testing campaign with equal budget. Kill underperformers at day 7 by CTR; kill survivors at day 14 by CPA. Scale the 2–3 winners into main campaigns.

4

Google Shopping Setup

Optimize product feed titles, descriptions, and custom labels for margin-based bidding. Structure Standard Shopping by category + PMax for catch-all. Feed quality is 70% of Shopping performance.

5

Retention Program Launch

Build Klaviyo welcome series (7-touch, 14-day), abandoned cart (3-touch with offer escalation), and post-purchase education flow. SMS added for high-intent recovery — abandoned cart SMS converts at 25–30%.

6

Scale & Monthly Optimization

Weekly budget pacing against ROAS target, monthly creative refresh, and quarterly channel expansion review. Blended ROAS improves as email/SMS revenue base grows — reducing the burden on paid acquisition.

FAQ

Ecommerce Marketing Questions

What ecommerce platforms do you work with?

Shopify and Shopify Plus are our primary platforms, but we also work with WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and headless commerce setups. Platform choice affects CAPI implementation, Klaviyo integration, and feed optimization strategy — we tailor our approach accordingly.

What ROAS can we realistically expect?

Blended ROAS (across all paid channels) of 3–4x is typical for mature ecommerce brands with an AOV above $50. New accounts spend the first 30 days in creative testing before scaling. Brands with strong email/SMS programs see blended ROAS improve 0.5–1x because retention revenue isn't attributed to paid.

How do you handle attribution after iOS 14?

We implement Meta Conversions API (CAPI), GA4, and a multi-touch attribution tool (Triple Whale or Northbeam) to reconstruct the full customer journey. Last-click reporting understates Meta and TikTok's contribution — blended ROAS with MTA gives you an accurate picture to make budget decisions.

Do you manage email and SMS, or just paid ads?

Both. Email/SMS (Klaviyo) is integral to ecommerce profitability — welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, and replenishment flows generate 30–40% of total revenue at near-zero marginal cost. We treat retention as a core channel, not an afterthought.

How long does it take to reach ROAS targets?

With an existing creative library and proven product-market fit, most ecommerce brands hit their blended ROAS target within 60 days. Days 1–30 focus on tracking setup and creative testing; days 30–60 scale proven winners. Email/SMS flows are live by day 14.
