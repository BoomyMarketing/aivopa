Our Process

How We Attract Premium Design Clients in 60 Days

1
Portfolio & Client Profile Audit

Review your best-performing project photography and identify project types that align with your ideal client budget, aesthetic, and geography. Define your target client: homeowner vs commercial, budget range, neighborhood, design style. This shapes every targeting and creative decision.

2
Platform Presence Audit

Assess your current Houzz profile, Instagram presence, Pinterest boards, and Google presence. Identify gaps vs competitors for similar-tier designers in NYC. Prioritize platform improvements that will generate the fastest ROI given your portfolio quality.

3
Visual Creative Selection

Select hero project images for paid ads — prioritizing photos that showcase finished spaces matching your target client budget and style. Brief any photography needs before launching campaigns. Creative quality is the single largest lever in interior design marketing performance.

4
Multi-Channel Launch

Launch Google Search for high-intent queries, Pinterest for early-stage inspiration, Instagram for portfolio visibility, and Houzz optimization simultaneously. Each channel serves a different stage of the client consideration journey.

5
Retargeting & Nurture

Retarget portfolio page visitors on Instagram and Google Display with additional project photography. Build email sequences for prospects who've downloaded your portfolio or requested a consultation but haven't committed. Maintain brand visibility throughout the 3–12 month consideration window.

6
Project Value Reporting

Monthly reporting on inquiries, consultations, and signed projects by channel — segmented by project budget range. Shift budget toward channels producing clients with the highest project budgets and best aesthetic fit, not just highest inquiry volume.
