How We Generate Orthodontic Patients in New York

Orthodontic patient acquisition in NYC requires targeting two distinct buyer profiles — adults seeking Invisalign and parents seeking braces for their children — with entirely different channels, creative, and conversion paths for each.

🔍
Google Search Ads
Intent-driven campaigns targeting "Invisalign NYC," "orthodontist Brooklyn," "braces for adults Manhattan," "kids orthodontist Queens," and "free orthodontic consultation New York" — patients actively researching treatment who have already committed to starting and are selecting a provider. We separate Invisalign campaigns (adult demographics, higher CPCs, lifestyle messaging) from braces campaigns (parent demographics, insurance focus, proximity messaging) with distinct landing pages and conversion flows for each. Free consultation offers in ad copy significantly increase click-through and booking rates by removing the financial uncertainty that delays orthodontic decisions.
📱
Meta Ads for Smile Transformation
Instagram and Facebook campaigns built around before-and-after smile transformation content — the most compelling creative format in orthodontics because treatment results are visually dramatic and immediately aspirational. Adult Invisalign campaigns target 25–45 year-old professionals with lifestyle messaging about clear aligners in workplace and social settings. Family campaigns target parents in NYC's family-dense outer boroughs with multi-child messaging and sibling discount offers. Video testimonials from completed patients — short 30-60 second stories shared in Reels and Stories — generate the highest conversion rates of any orthodontic creative format on Meta, outperforming product-feature ads by 2–4x in consultation booking rate.
📍
Local SEO & Google Business Profile
Map-pack rankings for "orthodontist near me," "Invisalign provider Brooklyn," and "braces [neighborhood]" — proximity is a major decision factor for orthodontic patients who will visit the practice 6–20 times over 18–36 months. GBP optimized with before-and-after photos, patient testimonials, treatment type listings, insurance network information, and consistent review generation. Local SEO content targeting NYC neighborhood searches ("orthodontist Park Slope," "Invisalign Astoria") builds organic visibility that compounds over time. Review velocity and recency are critical in orthodontics — patients research heavily before booking a $4,500–8,000 commitment, and practices with 100+ recent 5-star reviews consistently outconvert those with fewer or older reviews.
🎬
YouTube Patient Acquisition
YouTube pre-roll and in-feed ads targeting viewers who've searched orthodontic terms or watched dental/beauty content — reaching patients during the 2–6 week consideration phase before they book a consultation. Orthodontic content performs exceptionally well on YouTube because patients actively search for "Invisalign before and after," "what braces feel like," and "Invisalign vs braces" — educational content that positions your practice as knowledgeable while building familiarity that converts to consultation bookings. 60-90 second practice tour videos and orthodontist introduction content build the personal connection that makes patients choose you over a competitor whose name they only saw in an ad.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family Demographic Targeting
Families with multiple children are the highest-LTV segment in orthodontics — a family that starts treatment with one child often brings 2–3 siblings through treatment over 5–15 years, generating $10,000–25,000+ in total practice revenue per family. We build family-targeting campaigns using parental demographic signals (parents of children ages 8–17), school-district geographic targeting in NYC neighborhoods with high family density (Bayside, Riverdale, Staten Island, Forest Hills), and sibling discount messaging that incentivizes multi-case starts. Parent Facebook communities and neighborhood groups are high-value organic amplification channels that supplement paid campaigns at no marginal cost.
💳
Insurance & Financing Conversion
Cost is the most common reason prospective orthodontic patients delay or abandon the consultation process — prominently advertising insurance compatibility, 0% financing, and monthly payment options removes the financial barrier before it becomes an objection. We integrate insurance network status, financing offers, and free consultation availability into every ad, landing page, and booking flow — not buried in fine print but as primary value propositions. Practices that advertise "Insurance accepted · 0% financing available · Free consultation" consistently generate 30–50% higher consultation volume than practices with identical clinical quality that don't surface financial accessibility upfront.

Built for NYC Orthodontic Practices

Orthodontic patient acquisition in New York requires separate strategies for adult Invisalign and family braces, deep knowledge of NYC's family geography, and conversion systems that turn consultations into high-value, long-duration treatment starts.

01
Adult vs. Family Segmentation
Adult Invisalign patients and families seeking braces for children require completely different marketing approaches — adult patients are driven by aesthetics, lifestyle discretion, and professional identity; parents are driven by clinical outcomes, insurance compatibility, and geographic convenience for school-age children. Running both audiences in a single campaign dilutes messaging effectiveness for both segments. We build parallel campaign structures with distinct demographics, creative briefs, landing pages, and consultation booking flows — adult Invisalign campaigns that speak to career professionals wanting straighter teeth without visible hardware, and family campaigns that address parent concerns about treatment length, pain management, and practice atmosphere for children.
02
NYC Neighborhood Intelligence
Orthodontic demand in NYC is highly geography-dependent — family-dense outer borough neighborhoods (Bayside, Flushing, Riverdale, Forest Hills, Bensonhurst, Staten Island) are the highest-volume markets for pediatric and family orthodontics, while Manhattan's young professional population drives adult Invisalign demand. We structure campaigns with geographic bid adjustments and neighborhood-level targeting that concentrates spend in the demographics most likely to start treatment — parents within a 2–3 mile radius of family-oriented practices, and professional adults within commuting distance of midtown and downtown Manhattan locations. Wasted spend on non-converting geographies is one of the most common orthodontic marketing inefficiencies we eliminate.
03
Consultation-to-Start Optimization
The most common orthodontic marketing failure is generating consultation volume without treatment starts — patients who attend consultations and then don't start treatment represent real revenue loss, not just a conversion problem. We build post-consultation follow-up sequences (SMS, email, retargeting) that re-engage patients who attended a consultation but haven't confirmed a start date, address the most common objections (cost, timing, treatment type uncertainty) with targeted content, and provide practices with consultation conversion data that identifies where in the process patients are dropping off. Improving consultation-to-start rate from 25% to 35% on the same lead volume is worth $150K–400K in annual production for a mid-size NYC orthodontic practice.
