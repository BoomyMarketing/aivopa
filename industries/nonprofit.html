Our Process

How We Grow Nonprofit Impact

We start with what costs nothing — maximizing your Google Ad Grant — then build the donor acquisition and retention infrastructure that compounds over time.

1

Google Ad Grant Setup & Audit

Verify 501(c)(3) eligibility, apply for or audit existing grant account. Build campaign structure around mission-critical keyword categories: program awareness, donation intent, and volunteer recruitment. Fix compliance issues that cause grant accounts to be suspended (a common problem we resolve).

2

Donor Persona Development

Map your current donor demographics, gift size distribution, and acquisition sources. Build audience segments for Meta targeting: major donor prospects (high-income, philanthropic interest), mid-level donor prospects, and recurring donor candidates. Different segments get different messaging and asks.

3

Impact Story Creative

Identify 2–3 specific beneficiary stories or program outcomes to feature in campaigns. Concrete stories ("Maria received 6 months of job training and is now employed") outperform abstract mission statements. We brief and produce video and photo content around your most compelling impact narratives.

4

Email Fundraising Architecture

Build welcome series for new email subscribers (3-touch, 2-week sequence introducing mission and impact). Set up donation page abandonment sequence. Plan year-end campaign calendar: Giving Tuesday + 3 December touches + December 31 final appeal.

5

Monthly Donor Upgrade Program

Email and Meta campaigns converting one-time donors to monthly giving programs. Monthly donors have 5x higher LTV and dramatically smoother revenue for operating budgets. We target donors who gave 2+ times in the last 12 months — the highest-conversion segment for upgrade campaigns.

6

Reporting & Grant Compliance

Monthly reporting on Google Grant utilization, cost-per-donor by channel, email fundraising revenue, and year-over-year donor retention rate. Grant compliance reporting to ensure you never lose $10K/month in free advertising due to account policy violations.

FAQ

Nonprofit Marketing Questions

What is Google Ad Grants and how does it help nonprofits?

Google Ad Grants gives eligible 501(c)(3) nonprofits $10,000 per month in free Google Search advertising. Ads appear in Google search results for mission-relevant keywords — driving website traffic, donations, volunteer sign-ups, and program awareness at no cost. Grant accounts have specific requirements (minimum CTR, no single-word keywords, quality score minimums) that we manage to keep your account compliant and fully spending.

What types of nonprofits do you work with?

Social services, education and youth development, environmental organizations, arts and culture, healthcare nonprofits, faith-based organizations, advocacy groups, international NGOs, and community foundations. The marketing strategy varies significantly by mission, beneficiary audience, and primary goal (donations, volunteers, program participants, or policy advocacy).

How do you run donor acquisition campaigns on Meta?

Meta offers a 25% discount on ads for registered nonprofits through Meta's Charitable Giving Tools. We run donor acquisition campaigns targeting audiences who share demographic and interest profiles with your existing donor base. Story-driven video and impact photography consistently outperform generic 'donate now' messaging — we brief creative around specific beneficiary stories and measurable impact.

How important is email for nonprofit fundraising?

Email generates more nonprofit revenue than any other digital channel — typically 25–40% of online donations. We build welcome series for new subscribers, year-end giving campaign sequences (November–December is 30% of annual giving), and lapsed donor win-back campaigns. Monthly donor upgrade sequences turn one-time givers into recurring supporters with 5x higher LTV.

Can you help with year-end Giving Tuesday and holiday campaigns?

Yes — and this is where preparation matters most. Giving Tuesday and December campaigns require 8 weeks of advance setup: match gift partner secured, landing page built, email sequences written, Meta and Google campaigns pre-loaded. Organizations that plan in October raise 3–5x more than those who start in late November. We build the full campaign calendar in advance.
