How We Generate Fence Installation Leads in New York

Fence buyers research by material, style, and neighborhood — then compare contractors. Every campaign is built around matching the right fence type to the right buyer intent and converting project inquiries into booked estimates.

🔍
Google Search Ads
High-intent campaigns capturing "fence installation near me NYC," "wood fence contractor Brooklyn," and "vinyl fence installation Queens" — homeowners actively searching for contractors who are ready to get estimates. Campaigns structured by fence material and project type to match bids and copy to the specific search intent and job value.
✅
Local Services Ads (LSA)
Google Guaranteed badge at the top of fence installation searches — verification builds trust for on-property work where homeowners are inviting contractors to measure and install on their land. LSA charges per lead rather than per click, and the badge improves closing rates for residential fence projects across NYC's outer boroughs and surrounding suburbs.
🪵
Material-Specific Pages
Wood, vinyl, aluminum, chain-link, wrought iron, and composite fence buyers each search differently and have different price expectations. We build dedicated landing pages and ad groups for every fence material you install — with material-specific photography, pricing ranges, maintenance comparisons, and FAQs that answer the exact questions each buyer type researches before requesting a quote.
📍
Local SEO & Google Business Profile
Map-pack rankings for fence installation searches across NYC boroughs and Long Island — GBP optimized with project portfolio photos, fence type gallery, service area map, and online estimate request link. Borough-specific optimization captures the hyper-local searches (Staten Island fence contractor, Bronx chain-link fence) where local operators consistently outrank national chains.
🏢
Commercial Property Campaigns
Construction site security, warehouse perimeter, school property, and municipal fencing represent the highest-value fence contracts in New York. We build targeted campaigns for general contractors, property managers, and facilities departments — decision-makers who authorize $10K–50K+ fence projects and value licensed, bonded, and commercially-experienced contractors over residential-focused competitors.
📸
Visual Portfolio Campaigns
Completed fence projects are highly visual and location-specific — a beautiful cedar privacy fence in a Brooklyn backyard or a black aluminum perimeter fence in a Queens garden resonates immediately with nearby homeowners. We run project photography campaigns on Instagram, Facebook, and Houzz targeting homeowners in specific NYC neighborhoods who are in renovation mode and actively comparing fence styles and contractors.
