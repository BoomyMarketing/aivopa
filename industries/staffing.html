Our Process

How We Build Staffing Agency Pipeline in 60 Days

1
Specialty & ICP Definition

Define your target employer profile: industry vertical, company size, location, typical headcount, and hiring volume. Identify your highest-margin placements and build campaigns that attract more of those clients specifically.

2
LinkedIn Audience Build

Build LinkedIn audience segments by job title (HR Director, VP Talent, Hiring Manager) + industry + company size. For ABM: upload named account lists. For broad reach: use LinkedIn's talent-function targeting to reach hiring stakeholders across your target industries.

3
Google Search & Landing Pages

Launch Google Search campaigns with vertical-specific ad groups. Build dedicated landing pages for each specialty: "IT staffing NYC," "healthcare temp agency New York," etc. Landing pages speak to the employer's pain — unfilled roles, cost-per-hire, time-to-fill.

4
Content & Thought Leadership

Publish LinkedIn articles and posts on hiring trends, talent market insights, and industry-specific staffing challenges. Thought leadership content warms prospects during long sales cycles and generates inbound connection requests from target buyers.

5
Candidate Pipeline Campaigns

Launch parallel candidate attraction campaigns on Google and Meta. Set up application tracking to measure candidate pipeline depth by specialty. A full candidate bench is a competitive differentiator when pitching new employer clients.

6
Pipeline Review & Optimization

Monthly review of employer inquiries, discovery calls, proposals, and signed clients by channel. Shift budget toward channels producing the highest-margin employer clients. Expand vertical coverage once core campaigns are profitable.
