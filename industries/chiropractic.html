Our Process

How We Grow Your Chiropractic Practice in 30 Days

1
Specialty & Patient Profile Audit

Define your chiropractic specialties (sports, family, PI, wellness), primary conditions treated, and target patient demographics. Identify your highest-value patient types — recurring wellness patients, sports athletes, or PI cases — so campaigns prioritize the patients with the best lifetime value.

2
GBP & Review Gap Analysis

Benchmark your Google Business Profile against top 3 competitor practices in your neighborhood. Identify review count and rating gaps. GBP optimization is the fastest ROI activity — improvements show results within 30–60 days and generate organic new patient calls with no ongoing ad spend.

3
Condition-Segmented Campaign Launch

Launch Google Search campaigns segmented by condition: back pain, neck pain, sciatica, sports injury, headaches. Each ad group drives to a condition-specific landing page. Call tracking numbers for each source — GBP, Google Ads, and organic — connect every call to its campaign.

4
Review Generation System Setup

Implement automated post-visit text and email review requests — sent 24 hours after each appointment. Direct link to Google review form. Staff training on in-person review asks. Target: 5+ new Google reviews per month to steadily build GBP authority over competitors.

5
Meta Awareness & Retargeting

Launch Facebook and Instagram campaigns targeting adults in your service ZIP codes with pain and wellness interest signals. Retargeting for website visitors who viewed condition pages but didn't book. PI campaign launch if personal injury is a target patient type.

6
New Patient & Care Plan Attribution

Monthly reporting on new patients, first-visit revenue, and care plan conversion rate by channel. Identify which sources produce recurring care plan patients vs. one-time pain relief visits — shift budget toward channels producing the highest-LTV patient relationships.

FAQ

Chiropractic Marketing Questions

Google Search is the primary channel — patients searching 'chiropractor near me,' 'back pain doctor NYC,' 'neck pain chiropractor Brooklyn,' or 'sports chiropractor Manhattan' are in active pain and looking for immediate relief. These high-intent searches convert quickly to first appointments. Google Business Profile ranking in the local 3-pack is critical — most chiropractic searches include a map pack click. Local SEO drives organic appointments for neighborhood-specific queries. Meta campaigns work well for condition-specific awareness (posture, sciatica, sports recovery) and for reaching office workers experiencing desk-related pain in specific NYC ZIP codes.

Chiropractic practices grow in two ways: acquiring new patients and retaining them for ongoing care plans. New patient acquisition via Google Search and GBP handles the first visit. Retention — converting a one-time pain relief visit into a long-term wellness care relationship — is driven by in-office care plan presentations and patient education, not marketing. Most chiropractors generate $800–3,000+ annually per retained patient across regular adjustments, massage, and wellness visits. Marketing investment in new patient acquisition has the highest ROI when the practice has strong retention systems in place to maximize lifetime patient value.

Condition-specific marketing outperforms generic 'chiropractor NYC' campaigns because it matches patient search behavior exactly. The highest-volume conditions in NYC: back pain (lower and upper), neck pain, sciatica, headaches/migraines, car accident injury, sports injuries, and desk worker posture problems. We build separate landing pages and ad groups for each major condition — a patient searching 'sciatica treatment Brooklyn' should land on a sciatica-specific page with relevant case content, not a generic homepage. Condition specificity also allows for more compelling ad copy that speaks directly to the patient's pain point.

Personal injury (PI) and auto accident chiropractic cases have a distinct marketing strategy because the payer is a law firm or insurance company, not the patient directly. Google Search for 'car accident chiropractor NYC,' 'personal injury chiropractor New York,' and 'accident injury treatment Brooklyn' captures patients immediately after accidents who need documentation for insurance claims. Law firm referral relationships are the other major PI case source — building relationships with personal injury attorneys who refer their clients for chiropractic documentation generates consistent high-value case volume. PI cases often generate $3,000–8,000+ per case through insurance billing.

Google Search for chiropractic keywords in NYC costs $4–15 per click, with CPL ranging from $30–90 for new patient appointment requests. Local SEO and GBP generate organic appointments at zero cost-per-click once established. Most chiropractic practices budget $800–2,500/month in ad spend and see 15–40 new patient inquiries per month. A new patient on a 12-visit care plan ($600–1,500) covers months of marketing spend. We track new patient appointments, care plan conversions, and revenue by acquisition channel.
