Our Process

How We Generate Leads for Home Services

A 30-day activation sprint to get inbound calls flowing, then ongoing optimization to lower cost-per-booked-job month over month.

1

Service Area & Competitor Audit

Map your service area by borough/neighborhood, identify the CPCs and LSA competition for your service category, and audit your Google Business Profile. Most home services businesses have significant GBP gaps before we start.

2

LSA Setup & Google Guaranteed

Submit business credentials for Google Guaranteed verification: license, insurance, and background check. Configure service types, service areas, and weekly budget cap. LSA verification takes 1–3 weeks — we start this on day one.

3

Google Ads Campaign Build

Service-specific ad groups with intent-matched keywords. Call extensions with tracking numbers. Location targeting by borough with bid adjustments for highest-value neighborhoods. Ad scheduling to match your availability and peak demand hours.

4

GBP Optimization

Upload 20+ high-quality photos (work photos, truck, team), complete all service categories, set accurate hours (including emergency/24-hour if applicable), and respond to all existing reviews. Weekly GBP posts to signal active business to Google's local ranking algorithm.

5

Call Tracking & Reporting

Unique CallRail numbers for each campaign. Weekly call recording review to identify unqualified lead patterns (wrong service area, wrong service type) and refine negative keywords. Connect to your CRM or dispatch software for revenue-based optimization.

6

Monthly Optimization & Seasonal Planning

Monthly budget reallocation based on cost-per-booked-job by campaign. Seasonal budget ramp-up 2 weeks before peak periods. Quarterly expansion into new service types or boroughs as capacity allows.

FAQ

Home Services Marketing Questions

What home service categories do you work with?

Plumbing, HVAC (heating, ventilation, air conditioning), electrical, roofing, general contracting, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, landscaping, pest control, cleaning services, painting, and handyman services. Any service where customers search Google when they have an immediate need — we can generate leads.

What are Local Services Ads and are they right for my business?

Local Services Ads (LSA) are Google's pay-per-lead ad format with a 'Google Guaranteed' badge. They appear above regular Google Ads and Maps results. For home services, LSA is almost always the first channel we recommend — you only pay when a qualified lead calls or messages, and the Google Guaranteed badge builds immediate trust with homeowners.

How much does home services Google Ads cost in NYC?

Emergency and high-ticket services have the highest CPCs: plumbing ($15–$45/click), HVAC ($12–$40/click), roofing ($20–$60/click). General remodeling runs $8–$25/click. LSA cost-per-lead for home services typically runs $20–$80 depending on service type and competition in your borough.

How do you handle seasonal demand for HVAC and other seasonal services?

HVAC, roofing, and landscaping have predictable demand peaks. We pre-build campaigns for each season, increase budgets 2 weeks before peak periods, and schedule creative refreshes for seasonal messaging (AC tune-up in spring, furnace in fall). Off-peak periods focus on maintenance plans and review generation — building reputation before the next surge.

How do you measure results for home services campaigns?

Primary KPI: inbound calls and form submissions, tracked by campaign and keyword via CallRail. Secondary: cost-per-booked-job (from your CRM or dispatch software). We connect ad spend to job revenue so you know exactly which campaigns are profitable — not just which ones generate the most calls.
