Window Cleaning Marketing Questions

Google Search is the primary acquisition channel for window cleaning — "window cleaning NYC," "window washers near me," and "commercial window cleaning Manhattan" are high-intent searches from property managers and homeowners ready to hire. Local Services Ads (LSA) with the Google Guaranteed badge are particularly effective for residential window cleaning because the badge builds trust for in-home services and LSA leads are exclusive rather than shared. Google Business Profile and local SEO drive map-pack visibility for neighborhood discovery. For commercial accounts, direct outreach to property managers combined with Google Search campaigns targeting facility management keywords generates the highest-value contracts.
Commercial window cleaning contracts are worth $500–10K+ annually per account and require a different acquisition strategy than residential. We run Google Search campaigns targeting "commercial window cleaning NYC," "office building window service Manhattan," and "property management window cleaning" — searches made by facility managers and building superintendents with ongoing budget authority. LinkedIn campaigns targeting property managers and building owners in specific NYC submarkets (Class A office, residential co-ops, retail) generate direct outreach opportunities. A strong portfolio of recognized NYC building clients, detailed before/after photos, and insurance documentation on your website converts B2B inquiries into contracts significantly faster than residential calls.
Google LSA places your business at the very top of search results with a "Google Guaranteed" badge — above regular ads and organic results. For window cleaning, where clients are inviting workers into their home or building, the verified badge is a significant trust signal that increases click-through and conversion rates. LSA charges per lead rather than per click, and leads are phone calls or message inquiries from verified Google users. We manage the LSA profile, dispute invalid leads, and optimize your review volume (the primary ranking factor in LSA) to keep your cost per lead competitive in the NYC market.
Residential window cleaning clients naturally rebook 2–4 times per year — spring, fall, and after construction or renovation. We build seasonal reminder campaigns that prompt clients to schedule before the peak season fills up, and pre-winter campaigns that promote interior cleaning packages when exterior work slows. Commercial clients on monthly or quarterly contracts represent the most stable revenue — we position recurring service agreements as the primary offer in commercial campaigns. A loyalty discount for clients who pre-book all four annual cleanings reduces churn and creates a predictable revenue base that doesn't require constant new acquisition.

LSA CPL runs $20–60 per verified lead with better closing rates than Search clicks. Most window cleaning companies start with $500–2K/month in ad spend. With residential tickets of $200–600 per visit and commercial contracts worth $500–5K+ annually, even at $80 CPL the economics are strongly favorable.
