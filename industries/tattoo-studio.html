How We Generate Tattoo Bookings in New York

From first Instagram follow to booked consultation to loyal multi-piece client — every campaign is built around the portfolio-driven nature of tattoo marketing in New York's competitive studio scene.

📸
Instagram & TikTok Portfolio Campaigns
Paid promotion of your strongest portfolio pieces to local NYC audiences — targeting by age, neighborhood, and style affinity to attract the clients who are pre-sold on your specific aesthetic before they ever send a DM. Process videos, healed tattoo showcases, and artist behind-the-scenes content build the following that fills your consultation queue.
🔍
Google Search Ads
High-intent campaigns capturing "fine line tattoo artist NYC," "blackwork tattoo studio Brooklyn," and "tattoo shop near me Manhattan" — search intent that signals a client who has decided to get tattooed and is now choosing who will do it, making them the highest-converting traffic in the acquisition funnel.
📍
Local SEO & Google Business Profile
Map-pack rankings for tattoo searches in your neighborhood — GBP optimized with portfolio photos, artist profiles, style specialties, and consultation booking link so "tattoo near me" surfaces your studio first for walk-in inquiries and clients who discovered you through search rather than social.
🎨
Artist Personal Brand Campaigns
Individual artist Instagram growth campaigns that build a personal following separate from the studio — because clients in NYC book the artist, not the address. An artist with 20K followers who moves shops takes their waitlist with them, making personal brand equity the most valuable asset in tattoo marketing.
📋
Consultation Booking Funnel
Landing pages and Instagram link-in-bio flows optimized for consultation form submissions — capturing client name, tattoo concept, placement, size, and reference images before the first conversation so artists can prioritize inquiries that match their style and availability, reducing time spent on poor-fit consultations.
⭐
Review Generation
Post-session Google review requests that build your studio rating with detailed reviews mentioning style, artist name, and healing experience — the trust signals that convert hesitant first-timers who find you through search and need social proof beyond your portfolio to make the booking decision.

Portfolio-Driven Creative
Art quality is the only conversion driver that matters in tattoo marketing. Style Niche Positioning
Fine line, traditional Japanese, blackwork, neo-traditional, realism — every style has a different audience, different search terms, and different competitive landscape. Booked-out artists with 3-month waitlists and walk-in studios taking same-day appointments require opposite marketing strategies. We build the right system for your studio model — waitlist management and premium positioning for sought-after artists, high-volume local discovery and impulse conversion for walk-in shops focused on chair utilization.
