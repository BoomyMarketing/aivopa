Our Process

How We Build Startup Pipeline in 60 Days

1
ICP Definition Workshop

Define your ideal customer profile: industry, company size, job title, pain point, buying trigger, and competitive alternatives. ICP precision determines campaign efficiency — vague targeting wastes budget on leads that never close.

2
Keyword & Audience Research

Map Google Search volume for category, competitor, and problem-aware keywords. Build LinkedIn audiences by title, industry, and company size. Identify highest-intent segments to prioritize in month one.

3
Landing Page & Conversion Setup

Build or optimize dedicated landing pages for each campaign theme. Set up CRM integration, form tracking, call tracking, and UTM attribution before launch. Lead source tracking is non-negotiable from day one.

4
Campaign Launch

Launch Google Search and LinkedIn with narrow targeting and controlled budget. Run 3–5 creative variations per channel. First qualified leads arrive within 2–3 weeks. Week 2 review identifies winning audiences and copy themes.

5
SQL Feedback Loop

Weekly pipeline reviews with your sales team. We need to know which leads converted to SQLs and closed-won — this feedback drives budget allocation away from leads that don't convert regardless of CPL.

6
Scale & Expand

Double budget on highest-LTV channels. Add content marketing and SEO for compounding pipeline. Expand to new ICPs or geographies once core campaign is profitable. Monthly strategy reviews with roadmap to next growth stage.
