How We Generate Appliance Repair Leads in New York

Broken appliances create urgent, high-intent searches. Every campaign is built around capturing that urgency — same-day availability, trusted local technicians, and brand-specific targeting that matches the search to the job.

🔍
Google Search Ads
High-urgency campaigns capturing "washing machine repair NYC same day," "refrigerator repair Brooklyn," and "dishwasher technician near me" — homeowners searching when an appliance fails who need service today, not next week. Bid strategies prioritize call clicks and fast-response ad copy to convert immediate intent into booked appointments.

Local Services Ads (LSA)
Google Guaranteed badge at the top of results — the highest-trust placement for in-home services. LSA charges per lead rather than per click, and the verification badge is critical for appliance repair where clients are welcoming a technician into their kitchen or laundry room. Verified leads have higher booking rates and lower no-show rates than standard Search leads.

Samsung repair NYC, LG dryer repair Brooklyn, Sub-Zero service Manhattan — appliance brand searches are the highest-converting keyword category because intent is maximally specific. We build dedicated ad groups for every brand you service, with matching landing pages that confirm your expertise and stocking of OEM parts for that brand.

Map-pack rankings for appliance repair searches across NYC boroughs — GBP optimized with service photos, appliance type list, same-day booking link, and review response cadence. Property Manager Outreach
Landlords and property management companies with multi-unit buildings are the highest-LTV accounts in appliance repair — a 30-unit building generates ongoing repair calls year-round.
