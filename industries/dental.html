What We Cover

Dental Marketing Channels

NYC has more dentists per capita than almost any market in the US. Standing out requires being visible in Google Maps, ranking in search for the right procedures, and running ads that convert high-value cosmetic patients — not just filling the hygienist's chair with cleanings.

🦷

Google Search Ads

Service-specific campaigns: "dentist near me NYC", "cosmetic dentist Manhattan", "Invisalign cost New York", "dental implants Brooklyn". Separate campaigns for general dentistry, cosmetic services, and emergency dental — each with different CPCs and patient value profiles.

🏅

Local Services Ads (Google Screened)

Pay-per-lead dental ads with Google Screened badge above traditional search results. LSA for dentists requires credential verification — we manage the process. Typically the most cost-efficient new patient acquisition channel for general dental practices in NYC.

🗺️

Google Maps & Local SEO

GBP optimization: specialties, insurance accepted, photos, hours, and review generation strategy. Map Pack presence for "[dentist] near me" searches drives more new patient calls than any other local channel. Star rating improvement is the highest-ROI activity for most dental practices.

📱

Meta — Cosmetic Patient Campaigns

Before/after photo campaigns for veneers, Invisalign, and smile makeovers targeting high-income NYC demographics. Lead Form ads for free cosmetic consultation offers. Instagram placements for visual treatment showcases. Not the right channel for general dentistry — but highly effective for cosmetic cases.

📞

Call Tracking & Booking

CallRail tracking per campaign, call recording for front-desk quality review, and integration with Dentrix, Eaglesoft, or Zocdoc for appointment tracking. Knowing which campaign produced a booked appointment (not just a call) is the difference between profitable and wasteful ad spend.

⭐

Review Generation

Automated post-appointment review request sequences via SMS and email. Google reviews are the #1 ranking signal for dental Map Pack visibility. We help practices go from 40 reviews to 200+ in 6 months — compounding into significantly more organic new patient calls.

Our Process

How We Grow Dental Practices

A 30-day activation sprint builds all channels simultaneously — so you're capturing patients from search, maps, and LSA from month one.

1

Practice Audit & Goal Setting

Audit current GBP completeness, review score vs. local competitors, existing website quality, and insurance acceptance. Set target new patient volume by service category — cosmetic cases have 5–10x the LTV of general cleanings and should command proportionally higher ad spend.

2

LSA Setup & Verification

Submit dental license, malpractice insurance, and background check for Google Screened verification. Configure service types (general, cosmetic, emergency, specialty) and service areas. LSA is typically live within 2–3 weeks of submission.

3

Google Search Campaigns

Build service-specific campaigns with procedure-level ad groups. Call-only campaigns for mobile emergency dental searches. Location extensions showing your address and hours. Call tracking numbers per campaign for accurate attribution.

4

GBP Optimization

Upload 30+ photos (operatory, reception, before/afters, team), complete insurance list, add all specialty services, and respond to all reviews. Weekly GBP posts for Google local ranking signal. Start automated post-appointment review request program.

5

Meta Cosmetic Campaigns (if applicable)

For practices focused on cosmetic cases: launch before/after Instagram campaigns targeting NYC high-income zip codes. Free consultation offer as the CTA. Retarget website visitors who viewed cosmetic service pages but didn't book.

6

Monthly Reporting & Optimization

Weekly cost-per-new-patient by channel and service type. Monthly budget reallocation toward highest-LTV patient acquisition channels. Quarterly review of case mix — are we generating the right patients, not just any patients?

FAQ

Dental Marketing Questions

What dental specialties do you work with?

General dentistry, cosmetic dentistry (veneers, whitening, Invisalign), orthodontics, oral surgery, periodontics, endodontics, pediatric dentistry, and multi-location DSOs (Dental Service Organizations). Each specialty has different CPCs, patient value, and competitive dynamics — we tailor campaigns accordingly.

How much does dental Google Ads cost in NYC?

General dentistry and teeth whitening CPCs run $8–$25 per click. Cosmetic services (veneers, Invisalign, implants) range $25–$75 per click due to higher patient LTV. Implant campaigns can exceed $100/click in Manhattan. Local Services Ads for dentists run $30–$90 per verified lead — and the pay-per-lead model makes LSA a strong complement to Search campaigns.

How do you attract high-value cosmetic dental patients?

Cosmetic patients research extensively before choosing a provider. We combine Google Search campaigns for high-intent cosmetic queries (Invisalign cost NYC, porcelain veneers dentist Manhattan) with Meta before/after photo campaigns targeting high-income demographics. Landing pages feature dentist credentials, patient transformations, financing options, and a low-barrier CTA (free consultation).

How do you track new patient conversions?

We implement call tracking (CallRail) with recording, online booking conversion tracking (Zocdoc, Dentrix, or your practice management software), and form submission tracking. Monthly reporting shows cost-per-new-patient by service category — so you know whether your implant campaigns or general cleaning campaigns generate the best ROI.

Do you work with multi-location dental groups?

Yes. DSOs and multi-location groups need a centralized campaign structure with location-level customization. We build location-specific ad groups, local landing pages, and individual GBP optimization for each location — while maintaining brand consistency and shared budget efficiency across the group.
