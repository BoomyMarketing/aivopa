Our Process

How We Fill Your Booking Calendar in 30 Days

1
Treatment Menu & Client Profile Audit

Identify your highest-margin treatments, most popular services, and ideal client profile. Distinguish between quick-turn treatments (facials, massages) and higher-ticket services (laser, injectables). This determines which channels and campaigns to prioritize first for fastest revenue impact.

2
Review & Local Presence Baseline

Audit your Google Business Profile, Yelp, and Mindbody/Vagaro listings. Identify review gaps vs. competitors and optimize all profiles with complete service lists, photos, and current hours. A strong local presence baseline amplifies every paid campaign you run.

3
Google Search Campaign Launch

Launch treatment-specific Google Search campaigns targeting your highest-margin services first. Build dedicated landing pages for each major treatment category with booking CTAs, before/after imagery, provider credentials, and review highlights. Conversion tracking connects every booking to its campaign.

4
Meta Visual Campaign Build

Develop Instagram and Facebook creative — treatment videos, before/after carousels, spa atmosphere content. Launch awareness campaigns for cold audiences and retargeting for website visitors who didn't book. Build email lookalike audiences from your existing client list for efficient new client acquisition.

5
Membership Funnel & Seasonal Calendar

Build membership acquisition retargeting sequences for first-visit clients. Create seasonal campaign calendar for Mother's Day, Valentine's Day, and holiday gift card season — with creative, offers, and budget allocation prepared 4 weeks in advance of each peak.

6
Booking & Revenue Attribution

Monthly reporting on new clients by channel, revenue per acquisition source, membership conversion rate, and gift card revenue. Track repeat visit rate by acquisition channel — not all leads are equal, and the cheapest CPL often produces the lowest-LTV clients. Optimize toward revenue, not just bookings.

FAQ

Spa & Med Spa Marketing Questions

Google Search captures clients actively searching for specific treatments: 'facial NYC,' 'Botox med spa Manhattan,' 'deep tissue massage Brooklyn,' 'HydraFacial New York.' These are the highest-intent leads with the shortest path to booking. Meta (Instagram and Facebook) drives discovery for visual treatments — before/after content for skin treatments, atmosphere shots for luxury spas, and promotional offers. Local SEO and Google Business Profile generate consistent organic bookings from neighborhood searches. For med spas with higher-ticket treatments ($500–5,000+), Meta and Google retargeting of website visitors is especially effective.

Med spa marketing focuses on clinical outcomes and practitioner credentials — clients booking Botox, fillers, laser treatments, or body contouring are making higher-stakes decisions and research more thoroughly. We emphasize provider qualifications, before/after results, and specific treatment benefits. Day spa marketing is more discovery and gifting-driven — seasonal promotions, package deals, and gift card campaigns perform well, and Meta visual content builds brand awareness effectively. Med spas see 60% of bookings come from Google Search for specific treatment names; day spas see more search volume for general terms like 'spa near me' and neighborhood searches.

Reviews are the single most important trust factor for spa and med spa clients — more than 80% of new spa clients read reviews before booking, and a Google Business Profile with 100+ 4.7+ star reviews dramatically outperforms one with 20 reviews at the same rating. We build systematic review generation into every campaign: automated post-visit review request sequences, staff training on asking in-person, and response protocols for managing negative reviews professionally. A strong review base reduces CPL on Google Search and converts more organic discovery traffic without additional ad spend.

Membership and package campaigns perform best as retargeting — visitors who've already shown interest in your spa convert at 3–5x the rate of cold audiences. We build membership acquisition funnels: awareness ads driving to treatment pages, retargeting with membership value ads (monthly savings calculation, exclusive member perks), and limited-time enrollment offers that create urgency. For existing clients, email and SMS campaigns at the 30-day post-visit mark have the highest membership conversion rates. Membership clients typically generate 4–6x the annual revenue of single-visit clients.

Google Search for spa and med spa keywords in NYC costs $3–15 per click (facial/massage lower, Botox/laser higher), with CPL ranging from $25–80 for well-optimized campaigns. Meta campaigns for spa discovery run $15–50 CPL. Most NYC spas see $10–20 in lifetime client value for every $1 spent on marketing once retention is factored in. Gift card campaigns in November–December typically see 200–400% ROAS. We track bookings by service type, new vs. returning client split, and monthly revenue per channel.
