Our Process

How We Generate Qualified Mortgage Leads in 14 Days

1
Loan Type & Borrower Profile Audit

Define your loan specialties (purchase, refinance, jumbo, FHA, VA, DSCR, self-employed), target borrower profiles, and geographic focus within NYC. Identify loan types with the best conversion rates and commission — this shapes keyword targeting and creative messaging.

2
Competitive Keyword & Rate Landscape

Audit search volume and competition for mortgage keywords across NYC boroughs. Identify gaps where brokers outperform big banks — specialty loans, local expertise, speed, and access to multiple lenders. Build keyword architecture targeting high-intent, lower-competition queries.

3
Google Search & Landing Page Launch

Launch Google Search campaigns segmented by loan type and borrower intent. Build landing pages for each loan category with clear CTAs, rate transparency, and application forms — optimized for the 40% of mortgage leads who apply within 48 hours of clicking.

4
Meta Lead Generation Activation

Launch Facebook and Instagram lead gen campaigns for first-time buyer education and refinance awareness. A/B test lead form vs. landing page conversion paths. Build lookalike audiences from your past closed loan client list for highest-quality lead targeting.

5
Local SEO & Referral Partner Outreach

Optimize Google Business Profile and build borough-specific SEO content. Launch LinkedIn outreach to active NYC real estate agents with co-marketing offers. Set up co-branded referral pages for your top 10 agent partners within 60 days.

6
Pipeline Tracking & Rate Optimization

Monthly reporting on leads, applications, and closed loans by channel. Monitor rate environment and adjust campaign mix between purchase and refinance as market shifts. Scale budget to channels producing the most closed loans — not just the most leads.

Mortgage Broker Marketing Questions

Google Search is the highest-intent channel — borrowers searching 'mortgage broker NYC,' 'best mortgage rates New York,' or 'FHA loan broker Brooklyn' are actively comparing lenders. Meta campaigns are effective for first-time homebuyer education and refinance awareness, especially for reaching specific demographics by ZIP code and income level. Local SEO and Google Business Profile drive organic discovery for borrowers researching local options. Realtor referral partnerships build a consistent stream of pre-qualified purchase leads that don't require paid media cost-per-lead.

Rate environment dramatically shifts the mix of purchase vs. refinance volume and CPL. In high-rate environments, purchase leads dominate but overall volume is lower — we focus budget on purchase intent keywords and first-time buyer education. In dropping-rate environments, refinance demand spikes quickly — we prepare campaigns in advance and activate immediately when rate movement creates opportunity. CPL in mortgage can range from $30–120 per lead depending on loan type, competition, and current rate sentiment. We monitor rate trends and adjust campaign strategy monthly.

Mortgage brokers win on service, speed, and access to multiple lender options — advantages that big banks can't match. We build campaigns around these differentiators: 'access to 50+ lenders,' 'close in 21 days,' 'local NYC mortgage expert.' Local SEO and Google Business Profile reviews are especially powerful — borrowers trust local experts with strong review records over national brand advertising. Niche loan expertise (jumbo NYC co-op loans, FHA, VA, self-employed income) creates keyword categories where brokers dominate search results.

Realtor partnerships are the highest-LTV lead source for most mortgage brokers — a single referring agent can send 12–30 loans per year. We help build referral pipelines through LinkedIn campaigns targeting active NYC real estate agents, co-marketing content (market reports, buyer guides) you can share with agents, and local real estate event sponsorships. We also build co-branded landing pages for agent websites that generate direct mortgage referrals. Most brokers with an active referral marketing program see 30–50% of their volume from agent referrals within 12 months.

Google Search mortgage keywords in NYC are competitive — $8–25 per click — with CPL ranging from $40–120 for well-optimized campaigns. Meta lead gen for mortgage is more cost-efficient at $20–60 CPL but requires more lead qualification. Most mortgage brokers start with $1,500–4,000/month in ad spend plus management fees. Given that a closed loan generates $3,000–15,000+ in broker commission, a CPL of $50–100 is highly profitable at even modest close rates. We track applications submitted, approvals, and closed loans by channel.
