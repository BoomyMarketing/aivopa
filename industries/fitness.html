What We Cover

Fitness Marketing Channels

NYC is one of the most competitive fitness markets in the world — with boutique studios, big-box gyms, and online programs all competing for the same health-conscious consumers. Winning requires hyperlocal targeting, compelling offers, and creative that matches the energy of your brand.

Google Search — Local Intent

"Gym near me", "pilates studio NYC", "personal trainer Manhattan", "CrossFit Brooklyn" — high-intent searches from people ready to commit. We run Search campaigns with call extensions, location targeting by neighborhood radius, and ad scheduling aligned to your peak inquiry hours.

📱

Meta & Instagram Ads

Neighborhood-radius targeting for local studio campaigns. Transformation before/after content, class highlight reels, and trainer spotlights. Lead Form ads for free trial sign-ups that convert in-app without requiring a website visit. Retargeting for website visitors who didn't book.

🎵

TikTok Fitness Ads

Workout clips, studio atmosphere content, and trainer personality videos reach younger NYC fitness audiences. TikTok drives discovery and aspiration — top-of-funnel brand building that feeds downstream Google and Meta conversions. Spark Ads amplify organic content that performs.

🗺️

Google Maps & Local SEO

GBP optimization: class schedule, amenities, photos, and review generation. Map Pack rankings for "[studio type] near me" searches. Fitness businesses with 100+ Google reviews see 3x more Map Pack calls than those with fewer than 25.

🎁

Trial Offer Campaigns

Free first class, 7-day trial, or intro pack offers as the top-of-funnel CTA. Low-friction entry reduces barrier to commitment. We design the offer sequence — trial → nurture → full membership — and build the follow-up automation in email and SMS to maximize conversion.

📧

Email & SMS Retention

Trial nurture sequences, class reminder SMS, milestone emails (30/60/90-day member), and win-back campaigns for lapsed members. Retaining a member costs 5x less than acquiring a new one — retention programs have the highest ROI of any fitness marketing investment.

Our Process

How We Grow Fitness Businesses

A 30-day sprint to activate all channels, then seasonal optimization around January, spring, and pre-summer demand peaks.

1

Market & Competitor Audit

Map competing studios and gyms within a 1-mile radius. Analyze their Google reviews, GBP completeness, and visible ad creative. Find the positioning angle — price, specialty, community, convenience, or results — that differentiates your brand before spending a dollar.

2

Offer Strategy

Design the intro offer that drives trial conversion. Free first class works for boutique studios; 7-day trial pass works for gyms; $99 intro month works for premium brands. The offer determines the entire campaign economics — we test 2–3 offers in the first 30 days.

3

Google Search & Maps Launch

Local search campaigns with neighborhood radius targeting. GBP optimization — photos, class schedule, amenities. Call-only campaigns for mobile searches. Conversion tracking via booking integration (Mindbody, Glofox, or Pike13).

4

Meta & TikTok Creative

Source authentic content: class atmosphere, instructor spotlights, member transformations, and behind-the-scenes. Launch Meta Lead Form campaigns for trial sign-ups. TikTok Spark Ads for top-performing organic posts. Creative refreshed monthly.

5

Trial Nurture Sequence

Email and SMS sequence starting the moment someone signs up for a trial: welcome, what to expect, reminder, post-visit check-in, and membership offer. The 72 hours after a first class are the highest-conversion window — automate that touchpoint.

6

Seasonal Planning

Budget spike for January (New Year's resolution peak), spring (bikini season demand), and September (back-to-routine). Maintain lower budgets in summer and holidays. Monthly reporting on cost-per-trial, trial-to-member rate, and cost-per-new-member by channel.

FAQ

Fitness Marketing Questions

What fitness businesses do you work with?

Gyms and health clubs, boutique fitness studios (Pilates, yoga, cycling, HIIT, barre), CrossFit boxes, personal trainers, martial arts schools, wellness centers, online fitness programs and apps, and fitness DTC brands (supplements, equipment, apparel). Each has different membership economics and acquisition cost targets.

What's the most effective offer for fitness lead generation?

Free trial or first-class-free offers consistently outperform discount memberships for initial conversion. The goal is getting a prospect through the door — conversion to paid membership happens in person. We structure campaigns around a low-friction intro offer, then help you build the in-person sales process to convert trials to full members.

How do you handle the January fitness rush and seasonal demand?

January is the highest-traffic month for fitness searches — 'gym near me' searches spike 200%+ in the first two weeks. We pre-build campaigns in December, increase budgets January 1, and run aggressive intro offers through mid-January. We also run 'New Year's Resolution' retention campaigns to existing members, reducing churn during the busy period.

Can you help with online fitness programs or apps?

Yes. Online fitness programs and apps use a different acquisition model — app install campaigns, free trial subscriptions, and content marketing rather than local search. We run Meta and TikTok campaigns optimized on free trial starts and paid subscription conversions, with email/SMS nurture to move free users to paid.

How do you measure fitness marketing results?

For studios and gyms: cost-per-trial, trial-to-member conversion rate, and cost-per-new-member. For online programs: cost-per-trial-start and cost-per-paid-subscriber. We integrate with Mindbody, Glofox, or your booking software to track which campaigns produce paying members — not just trial sign-ups.
