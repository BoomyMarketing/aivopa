Our Process

How We Scale Beauty Brands

A 60-day sprint to ROAS target — creative testing in the first 30 days, scaling in the next 30, retention compounding from month one.

1

Category & Competitor Audit

Analyze top beauty brands in your subcategory on Meta Ad Library, TikTok Creative Center, and Google Shopping. Map their hooks, formats, claims, and price anchoring to find your creative differentiation angle.

2

Tracking & Attribution Setup

Implement Meta CAPI, GA4, and Triple Whale (or Northbeam) to get accurate attribution in a post-iOS world. Beauty has high multi-touch journeys — last-click lies. We build blended ROAS as the north star.

3

UGC Creative Sprint

Source 6–10 creators, brief 3–5 concepts each, produce 20–30 unique ad variants. Test across 5 hooks in a dedicated Creative Testing campaign. Kill what doesn't work by day 14, scale what does.

4

Scaling Winning Creative

Move top performers into Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns (ASC) or broad targeting with campaign budget optimization. Refresh creative monthly — no beauty ad stays "fresh" past 4–6 weeks.

5

TikTok Shop Flywheel

Recruit 20–50 affiliate creators to post organically to TikTok Shop. Pay commission on sales only. Take the best-performing organic posts and amplify as Spark Ads — paid reach on proven content.

6

Retention & LTV Expansion

Build Klaviyo flows for post-purchase, replenishment (skincare has natural reorder cycles), and cross-sell between product lines. Beauty LTV hinges on second purchase rate — we track and optimize it explicitly.

FAQ

Beauty Marketing Questions

What beauty sub-categories do you work with?

Skincare (serums, SPF, anti-aging, acne), color cosmetics, haircare and scalp health, fragrance, nail, body care, clean beauty, and adjacent wellness categories like supplements, gummies, and personal care.

How do you approach TikTok Shop for beauty brands?

We set up and optimize TikTok Shop storefronts, manage the affiliate creator program (recruiting creators to post and earn commission on sales), and run TikTok Ads that drive directly to Shop PDPs. This flywheel — affiliate content feeding paid amplification — is the fastest way to grow GMV on TikTok for beauty brands.

What kind of UGC content performs best for beauty?

Before/after transformation videos, skin-of-color showcases, founder story hooks, and routine integration clips (showing how the product fits into a daily routine) consistently outperform studio product shots. We source creators who match your target demographics and brief them on proven hook structures.

Do you manage influencer partnerships or just paid ads?

Both. We handle influencer sourcing (micro and macro), contract negotiation, brief creation, content approval, whitelisting setup, and performance tracking. Influencer content is then used as paid dark-post creative — the highest-performing creative format for beauty on Meta and TikTok.

How quickly can a beauty brand reach a 3x ROAS target?

With proven creative and an AOV above $45, most beauty brands reach their blended ROAS target within 60 days. The first 30 days are dedicated to creative testing (5–8 concepts); days 30–60 scale the 2–3 winners. Email/SMS retention layers add 25–35% incremental revenue without touching ad spend.
