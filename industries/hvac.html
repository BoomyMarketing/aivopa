Google Search and LSA (Local Services Ads / Google Guaranteed) are the top channels for HVAC contractors. Search captures high-intent queries like "AC repair NYC" and "emergency furnace repair." LSA puts your business at the very top of results with a Google badge and a pay-per-lead model. Local SEO and Google Business Profile drive organic map-pack visibility year-round. Meta ads work for off-season maintenance plan promotions and retargeting past customers before the next season.
Seasonal surge campaigns launched 3–4 weeks before peak heat or cold spikes are critical. In New York, AC season runs June–August and heating season runs November–February. We pre-load budgets, expand keyword sets to include emergency and same-day terms, and raise bids on high-intent queries as temperatures shift. LSA budgets are increased during heat waves and cold snaps when demand spikes. Retargeting past customers for tune-ups before season start also fills the calendar before competitors ramp up.
Yes — emergency HVAC is the highest-converting segment. Commercial accounts typically involve longer sales cycles but produce $10K–100K+ annual contracts that anchor your revenue base.
