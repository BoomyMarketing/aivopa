FAQ

Florist Marketing Questions

Google Search captures bottom-of-funnel intent — queries like "wedding florist NYC" or "flower delivery Manhattan" convert directly. Pinterest is the discovery platform for floral design, especially for brides building wedding mood boards 6–18 months before the date. Instagram and Meta ads build visual brand equity and work for retargeting visitors who looked at your portfolio. Local SEO and Google Business Profile are essential for local delivery and walk-in queries, while seasonal Google Search campaigns around Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, and the fall wedding season generate the highest revenue spikes.
Wedding florist marketing in NYC requires presence at multiple touchpoints in the 6–18 month planning journey. Pinterest boards are where most brides start — pins that appear on "wedding centerpiece NYC" or "romantic wedding arch New York" boards generate inquiry requests months before the date. Google Search with wedding-specific campaigns (bridal bouquet, ceremony arch, floral table design NYC) captures planners actively comparing vendors. Portfolio-forward landing pages with real NYC wedding photos and detailed inquiry forms convert at the highest rate. We also target engaged couples on Instagram using life event targeting.
Valentine's Day and Mother's Day are the two highest-revenue days for NYC florists and require advance campaign preparation. We launch Google Search campaigns targeting "Valentine's Day flowers NYC" and "Mother's Day bouquet delivery Manhattan" 3–4 weeks before the holiday, then increase bids in the final 48–72 hours as urgency peaks. Google Shopping for specific arrangements, Meta retargeting for past customers, and email sequences to existing buyer lists drive repeat orders. Same-day delivery messaging in ad copy significantly increases conversion during the final 24 hours.
Corporate accounts — weekly office arrangements, event florals for corporate functions, and standing delivery contracts — are the most stable revenue for NYC florists. We use LinkedIn outreach targeting office managers, executive assistants, and event planners at Manhattan-based companies, alongside Google Search campaigns targeting "corporate flower delivery NYC" and "office plant rental Manhattan." Once a corporate client is acquired, the LTV can reach $5K–50K+ annually. We also target hotel event coordinators and wedding planners who refer high-value event floral business.
Florist CPCs on Google Search range from $2–8 for local delivery terms and $5–20 for wedding-specific queries. Pinterest CPMs run lower at $5–15, making it cost-efficient for visual discovery. Most NYC florists start with $1.5K–4K/month in ad spend and scale aggressively around Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, and fall wedding season. Cost per inquiry typically runs $25–80 for local delivery and $60–200 for wedding consultations. Vora's management fees are separate from ad spend and scale with campaign complexity and seasonality.
