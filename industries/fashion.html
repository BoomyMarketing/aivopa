Our Process

How We Scale Fashion Brands

Fashion marketing requires aesthetic alignment, seasonal planning, and performance discipline — simultaneously. Our process builds all three from week one.

1

Brand & Competitive Audit

Analyze your top-performing SKUs, current customer demographics, and creative from competing brands on Meta Ad Library and TikTok Creative Center. Find the visual differentiation angle before spending a dollar.

2

Tracking & Attribution Setup

Meta CAPI, GA4 e-commerce events, and Klaviyo integration. For fashion, purchase value tracking matters more than order count — we optimize campaigns on revenue, not clicks.

3

Influencer Sourcing & Briefing

Source 5–10 fashion creators who match your brand's visual language. Brief them on product story, styling direction, and key talking points. Their content is produced in parallel with paid campaign setup — ready to whitelist on launch day.

4

Paid Campaign Launch

Launch Meta Advantage+ Shopping and Google Shopping simultaneously. Use influencer content as Meta dark posts from day one — it outperforms brand studio creative in 80% of tests. TikTok Spark Ads launched as soon as influencer posts go live.

5

Email & SMS Retention

Klaviyo welcome series (collection-specific, not generic), abandoned cart with product images, post-purchase style guide, and VIP early-access program. Size-specific restock alerts are the highest-converting automation for apparel.

6

Seasonal Campaign Planning

6-week advance planning for each season drop. Creative brief at week 1, influencer sourcing at week 2, production weeks 3–4, ad setup week 5, launch week 6. Build the full SS/FW calendar at the start of each season.

FAQ

Fashion Marketing Questions

What fashion categories do you work with?

Contemporary and mid-market apparel, luxury and premium fashion, streetwear and sneakers, activewear and athleisure, accessories (bags, jewelry, shoes), and fashion-adjacent categories like home textiles and lifestyle brands. We adapt creative strategy to match your price point and brand positioning.

How do you balance brand-building and performance for fashion?

Fashion requires both. Performance campaigns (Advantage+ Shopping, Google Shopping) capture in-market buyers; brand campaigns (broad targeting on Meta/Instagram, TikTok awareness) build the desire that makes performance campaigns work. We run both simultaneously with separate budgets and KPIs — brand campaigns are measured on CPM efficiency and reach, not ROAS.

How do you handle seasonal campaigns for fashion brands?

Fashion runs on a seasonal calendar — Spring/Summer, Fall/Winter, and retail moments (NYFW, Black Friday, holiday, end-of-season sale). We plan campaigns 6 weeks in advance: creative briefing at week 1, production at weeks 2–4, audience and targeting setup at week 5, go-live at week 6. No scrambling the week of launch.

Can you work with luxury or high-price-point fashion brands?

Yes. Luxury fashion requires a different performance approach — longer consideration cycles, higher-touch creative, curated influencer partnerships, and exclusivity signals in ad copy. We avoid discount-driven creative and optimize on revenue value, not order volume. Audience targeting focuses on high-income, high-LTV customer segments.

What role does influencer marketing play in fashion?

For fashion, influencer content IS the creative. We source fashion creators whose aesthetic matches your brand — not just follower count — and use their content as both organic posts and whitelisted paid dark posts. Influencer-sourced UGC converts 3–5x better than studio photography for most fashion brands on TikTok and Meta.
