What We Cover

Education Marketing Channels

Education purchases are high-consideration, high-stakes decisions. Prospective students research intensively before applying — comparing programs, reading reviews, watching student testimonials, and evaluating career outcomes. We build presence at every stage of that journey.

🎓

Google Search — Enrollment Campaigns

Program-specific and career-outcome search campaigns: "[program] bootcamp NYC", "best MBA program new york", "learn [skill] online". Separate campaigns by program type, degree level, and delivery format (in-person vs. online). Call and form extensions for immediate inquiry.

📱

Meta Lead Generation

Facebook and Instagram campaigns targeting prospective students by age, education level, career interest, and life stage. Meta Lead Forms capture inquiry directly in-app — higher completion rate than driving to a website form. Retargeting sequences for nurture.

🔍

Education SEO

Program pages optimized for "[degree/certificate] in [city]" and career-outcome queries ("become a data scientist NYC"). Student testimonials and outcome data as ranking signals. Technical SEO for multi-program institution websites with complex URL structures.

🎬

YouTube & Video Advertising

Student success story videos, virtual campus tours, and program overview content served to in-market audiences on YouTube. Video builds trust and emotional connection during the long consideration phase — especially effective for higher-cost programs.

📧

Enrollment Nurture Sequences

Email and SMS sequences that move leads from inquiry to application to enrollment. Program-specific drip campaigns with outcome data, financial aid information, and deadline reminders. Nurture is critical for programs with 3–12 month consideration cycles.

📊

CRM & Enrollment Attribution

Connect ad platforms to your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, or Slate for higher ed) to track leads from first inquiry through application, acceptance, and enrollment. Optimize campaigns on enrolled students, not just inquiries.

Our Process

How We Grow Enrollment

Education marketing runs on enrollment cycles — semester starts, application deadlines, and financial aid windows. We build campaigns around your calendar, not a generic template.

1

Program & Audience Mapping

Map each program to its target student persona, career outcome, and competitive alternatives. Different programs need different messaging — a coding bootcamp targeting career-changers sounds nothing like an executive MBA targeting mid-career professionals.

2

Landing Page Audit & Build

One landing page per program — not your homepage. Page must include: program outcomes, curriculum highlights, student testimonials, financial aid info, and a clear CTA (request info or apply now). Generic landing pages waste budget.

3

Campaign Architecture

Separate campaigns by program, delivery format, and funnel stage. Top-of-funnel awareness campaigns (YouTube, broad Meta) feed middle-funnel consideration campaigns (search intent, retargeting) which feed bottom-funnel conversion campaigns (application deadline messaging).

4

CRM Integration

Connect Google Ads and Meta to your CRM. Import offline enrollment data to train smart bidding algorithms on actual enrolled students, not just web form submissions. This shift alone typically improves CPE (cost-per-enrollment) by 20–40%.

5

Nurture Sequence Setup

Build email and SMS drip sequences for each inquiry source and program. Bootcamp leads get an 8-week sequence; graduate program leads get a 6-month sequence. Automated sequences keep your program top-of-mind without manual follow-up.

6

Enrollment Cycle Optimization

Budget spikes 6–8 weeks before application deadlines. Scale back after deadline, shift to next intake. Monthly reporting on CPL, application rate, and CPE by program and channel. Quarterly budget reallocation based on cost-per-enrolled-student.

FAQ

Education Marketing Questions

What education categories do you work with?

Higher education (universities and colleges), graduate and professional programs, coding bootcamps and vocational training, K–12 private schools, online course creators, tutoring and test prep, language schools, and edtech SaaS platforms targeting institutions or learners directly.

How do you generate qualified enrollment inquiries?

We run Google Search campaigns targeting program-specific and career-outcome queries ('data science bootcamp NYC', 'MBA programs new york', 'learn UX design online'). Meta campaigns reach prospective students by age, education level, and career interest. Lead quality is measured by application completion and enrollment rate, not just form fills.

How do you handle Meta advertising restrictions for education?

Meta classifies some education advertising under Special Ad Categories (employment, housing, credit — but not education directly). However, targeting limitations apply to financial aid or income-related messaging. We structure campaigns to maximize targeting precision within platform policies and focus on academic and career interest signals.

How long does the education enrollment funnel typically take?

Bootcamp and short-course enrollment cycles run 2–8 weeks from first inquiry to enrollment decision. University and graduate program cycles run 3–12 months. We build nurture sequences (email, retargeting) that match the length of your program's consideration cycle — not just one-touch lead capture.

Can you help with both domestic and international student recruitment?

Yes. Domestic recruitment focuses on Google Search and Meta with career-outcome messaging. International student recruitment adds YouTube, country-specific geo-targeting, and WeChat/LINE advertising for key source markets. We build separate campaign structures for each audience with messaging aligned to their specific motivations and visa pathways.
