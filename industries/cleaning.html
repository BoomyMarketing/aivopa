Our Process

How We Build Your Cleaning Client Base in 30 Days

1
Service Area & Capacity Audit

Map your service areas by borough and neighborhood. Define capacity limits (teams, vehicles, time slots) so we don't generate more leads than you can service. Identify your most profitable service types to prioritize in campaigns.

2
LSA Setup & Verification

Navigate Google LSA background check, insurance verification, and license requirements for cleaning businesses in New York. Profile setup with service categories, photos, and business hours optimized for booking intent.

3
Google Search & Landing Page Launch

Launch campaigns with service-segmented ad groups. Build landing pages with online instant quote or booking CTA, trust signals (insured, bonded, background-checked), and recurring plan offer prominently featured.

4
Review Generation System

Set up automated post-clean SMS review requests. Train your team on optimal timing (2–4 hours after service) and follow-up protocol. Target 5–10 new reviews per week to build volume ahead of competitors.

5
Recurring Upgrade Sequences

Post-first-booking email/SMS sequence: thank you, quality check-in, recurring plan offer (10% off biweekly), loyalty reward at booking #3. Each touchpoint moves one-time clients toward recurring subscription.

6
Reporting & Neighborhood Optimization

Monthly reporting on leads, first bookings, recurring conversions, and revenue by campaign. Expand or contract service area targeting based on route efficiency and job density. Shift budget toward neighborhoods with highest recurring conversion rates.
