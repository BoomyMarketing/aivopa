Plumbing Marketing Questions

Google Search is the dominant channel for plumbing — emergency and urgent searches like 'plumber near me,' 'emergency plumber NYC,' and 'burst pipe repair Brooklyn' happen at any hour and require immediate response. Local Services Ads (LSA) are especially powerful for plumbers because the Google Guaranteed badge addresses the trust barrier in a high-urgency purchase. Local SEO and Google Maps ranking ensures your business appears in neighborhood searches. 24/7 call coverage is essential — plumbing emergencies don't follow business hours.

Emergency plumbing marketing requires 24/7 ad scheduling, 24/7 call answering (or answering service), and ad copy that emphasizes immediate availability: 'available 24/7,' 'same-day service,' 'emergency plumber NYC — call now.' We run full-day campaigns with bid adjustments that increase bids during peak emergency hours (6pm–midnight and 6–9am) while maintaining presence overnight. Call tracking shows after-hours volume so you can evaluate whether 24/7 staffing pays for itself.

LSA for plumbers display above standard Google results with a Google Guaranteed badge. You pay per verified lead (call or message), not per click — typically $15–40 per plumbing lead in NYC. The Google Guaranteed badge is critical for plumbing because homeowners want to know the plumber entering their home is vetted. LSA requires background check and license verification, which we navigate. We manage your profile, set your service hours, and dispute invalid leads that don't meet lead quality standards.

Both serve different revenue streams. Emergency repair searches are highest-intent and highest-urgency — the homeowner will call whoever appears first and can come immediately. Scheduled maintenance and installation campaigns (water heater replacement, drain cleaning, pipe repiping) have longer consideration cycles but higher average job values. We build separate campaigns for each category: emergency campaigns optimized for speed-to-answer, maintenance campaigns with financing offers and diagnostic specials. The mix depends on your capacity and preferred job types.

Google Search and LSA generate first inbound calls within hours of launch — sometimes within the first hour for emergency plumbing queries in NYC. CPCs for plumbing keywords in NYC range from $10–35 per click, with CPL typically $35–100 for emergency and service calls. Most plumbing companies see 30–80 leads per month at moderate ad budgets. The key metric is cost-per-booked-job, not just cost-per-call — we track which calls convert to scheduled appointments and completed jobs.
