How We Generate Med-Spa Bookings in New York

Aesthetic patients in NYC research treatments extensively before booking — on Google, Instagram, and through peer recommendations. Every campaign is built to capture high-intent searches, build visual trust through social proof, and retain patients through membership and reactivation sequences.

🔍
Google Search Ads
High-intent campaigns targeting "Botox NYC," "lip filler Manhattan," "laser hair removal Brooklyn," "CoolSculpting New York," and "Morpheus8 NYC" — patients who have already researched the treatment and are evaluating which provider to book. Campaigns are structured by treatment type with separate budgets and bid strategies matched to the revenue value of each service — Botox and filler campaigns operate at higher CPCs than laser campaigns because the per-visit value and repeat frequency justify higher acquisition investment.
📱
Meta & Instagram Ads
Visual aesthetic campaigns on Instagram and Facebook — the platform where NYC aesthetic patients discover new treatments, compare providers, and form brand impressions before they search. We build campaigns around compliant lifestyle creative, practitioner spotlight content, patient testimonials, and treatment education videos that build awareness and intent among lookalike audiences of your existing patients. Instagram Reels and Stories perform especially well for non-invasive body contouring and skin treatment campaigns targeting NYC's 28–50 female demographic.
📍
Local SEO & Google Business Profile
Map-pack rankings for aesthetic searches across NYC — GBP optimized with treatment menus, before-and-after photos (where platform-compliant), practitioner credentials, and review solicitation sequences. Local SEO content targeting treatment + neighborhood searches ("Botox Upper East Side," "laser hair removal Midtown Manhattan") builds organic visibility that compounds over time. Review volume and recency are particularly influential in aesthetic medicine — patients read reviews carefully before trusting a provider with injectables or laser treatments.
🔄
Retargeting & Funnel Sequences
Aesthetic patients typically research for weeks before booking — visiting your website multiple times, comparing providers, watching videos, and reading reviews before making a decision. We build retargeting sequences that keep your med-spa top-of-mind during this consideration window: Google Display, Meta retargeting, and YouTube pre-roll campaigns that follow website visitors with treatment-specific messaging, practitioner credentialing, and offers (complimentary consultations, first-visit discounts) that convert hesitant prospects into booked appointments.
✉️
Email & SMS Patient Retention
Existing patients are your highest-ROI marketing asset — a patient retained for four annual Botox visits generates $3,200–10,000/year with $0 acquisition cost after the first visit. We build automated retention sequences: treatment maintenance reminders (sent 10–12 weeks post-Botox when the product begins wearing off), seasonal promotion campaigns (summer body contouring push, fall skin resurfacing season), birthday treatment offers, and lapsed patient reactivation sequences targeting patients who haven't booked in 6+ months.
💳
Membership & Package Campaigns
Monthly membership programs ($99–299/month for discounted treatments and priority booking) are the most powerful revenue model in med-spa — they convert episodic treatment buyers into predictable recurring revenue and increase treatment frequency by removing the per-visit payment friction. We build campaigns specifically designed to convert existing patients into membership subscribers and new patients into membership sign-ups at the first visit, using treatment bundles and multi-visit packages as the entry point for the membership conversation.

Built for NYC Medical Spas and Aesthetic Treatment Providers

Med-spa marketing in New York requires compliance expertise, visual creative that converts, and retention systems that turn one-time patients into lifetime clients. We build the full acquisition and retention stack that grows your bookings and your monthly recurring revenue.

01
Aesthetic-Compliant Creative
Meta and Google have strict policies around medical aesthetic advertising — before-and-after claims, procedure terminology, and health benefit statements trigger ad disapprovals and account restrictions that interrupt campaign momentum. We build campaigns that achieve high visual impact and conversion rates while remaining fully compliant with platform policies: lifestyle-focused creative, educational content, and patient testimonials structured to avoid the specific triggers that get aesthetic ads flagged. Compliance expertise means your campaigns run consistently without disruption from platform rejections that competitors experience regularly.
02
Treatment-Level Campaign Architecture
Botox, filler, laser hair removal, CoolSculpting, and skin resurfacing attract different patient profiles, have different search behaviors, different competitive intensity, and different revenue values per booking — running them in a single campaign blends signals and misallocates budget. We build separate campaigns for each treatment category with bid strategies matched to the revenue potential: higher bids for high-LTV filler and injectable patients who return every 3–4 months, more conservative bids for single-session treatments. This structure also allows precise creative matching — showing laser hair removal ads to laser searchers, not to Botox searchers who have different concerns and decision criteria.
03
Retention Revenue Focus
Most med-spa marketing focuses entirely on new patient acquisition — which is the most expensive part of the revenue equation. We build the retention layer that most agencies ignore: automated treatment maintenance reminders that reach patients exactly when their Botox is wearing off, seasonal campaigns that drive existing patients back for new treatments, and membership conversion sequences that transform one-time visitors into monthly recurring revenue. A med-spa with 200 retained patients booking 3× annually generates $480K–1.5M in annual revenue from patients who cost nothing to acquire after the first visit.

Google Search captures the highest-intent med-spa buyers — people searching 'Botox NYC,' 'lip filler Manhattan,' 'laser hair removal Brooklyn,' or 'CoolSculpting New York' have already researched the treatment and are evaluating which provider to book. Meta and Instagram ads are equally important because aesthetic patients are heavily influenced by visual content — before-and-after results, practitioner spotlights, and patient testimonial videos drive awareness and intent among audiences who haven't yet searched. Google Business Profile drives map-pack visibility for location-specific searches. Email and SMS retention sequences are the highest-ROI channel for med-spas because existing patients spending $800–2,500 per visit are far more valuable to reactivate than acquiring new patients at $40–120 CPL.
Meta has strict policies around before-and-after imagery, claims about medical procedures, and targeting for health-related services — campaigns that violate these policies get disapproved or accounts flagged, creating disruption that competitors exploit. We build Meta and Instagram campaigns that work within platform guidelines: lifestyle creative that shows confidence and results without explicit before-and-after comparisons, educational content about treatment processes rather than outcome claims, and UGC from patients that's clearly marked and compliant. Google has fewer restrictions on aesthetic treatment advertising than Meta, which is why search campaigns should anchor your med-spa acquisition strategy, with Meta and Instagram in a brand-building and retargeting role rather than direct response.
Botox and filler patients require re-treatment every 3–6 months to maintain results — a patient who comes in once is worth $800–2,500 per visit, but a retained patient who books four times per year generates $3,200–10,000 annually with near-zero additional acquisition cost. We build post-visit retention sequences that remind patients about maintenance timing, promote seasonal treatment bundles, and present membership programs ($99–299/month for discounted treatments and priority booking) that lock in recurring revenue. Membership revenue is the most predictable income in med-spa — a 100-member program at $150/month generates $180K/year in recurring revenue independent of new patient acquisition.
Dermatology offices and plastic surgeons have clinical credibility but limited marketing agility — their brands are built on physician authority and referral networks, not on the convenience, experience, and accessible pricing that med-spa patients increasingly prefer. Med-spas win by competing on experience (spa environment vs. clinical setting), scheduling accessibility (same-week or weekend appointments vs. 4–8 week waits at dermatology offices), transparent pricing (published menus vs. opaque insurance-adjacent billing), and ongoing relationship (membership model vs. episodic clinical visits). We build campaigns that lead with these advantages — positioning your med-spa as the expert provider for patients who want professional aesthetic results without the friction of a physician practice.
