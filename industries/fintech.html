Our Process

How We Run Fintech Campaigns

Every fintech engagement starts with compliance mapping before a single ad goes live. Then we build conversion infrastructure tuned to the metrics that actually matter — funded accounts, activated users, or qualified pipeline.

1

Compliance Audit

Map platform policies (Google, Meta, LinkedIn) against your product category. Identify required disclosures, restricted terms, and ad format limitations before creative development starts.

2

Conversion Architecture

Implement server-side CAPI and GA4 to track account opens, app installs, application completions, and funded accounts — events that require backend data, not just pixel fires.

3

Audience & Persona Build

Layer financial intent signals, fintech app behavior data, and firmographic targeting (for B2B) to build audiences that convert at acquisition cost targets — not just click.

4

Compliant Creative Production

Ad copy reviewed against disclosure requirements. No banned terms, proper APR/fee language, no performance guarantees for investment products. Fast creative iteration within guardrails.

5

Channel Diversification

Reduce platform concentration risk across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, programmatic DSPs, and CTV. Fintech brands that rely on one channel are one policy change away from a traffic cliff.

6

CAC / LTV Reporting

Weekly reporting on CAC by channel and cohort, payback period, and LTV projections. Not vanity metrics — unit economics that map to your fundraising or profitability milestones.

FAQ

Fintech Marketing Questions

Do you handle compliance requirements for financial advertising?

Yes. We work within FINRA, SEC, and platform-specific financial ad policies — including required disclosures, credit product restrictions, and crypto advertising rules on Meta and Google. Every ad is reviewed for compliance before launch.

What fintech verticals do you serve?

Neobanks and challenger banks, lending platforms (personal, SMB, BNPL), B2B fintech and payments infrastructure, crypto and Web3 products, insurtech, and wealth management / robo-advisory platforms.

How do you track conversions for regulated financial products?

We implement server-side Conversions API to track account opens, application completions, and funded accounts without over-relying on browser cookies. We exclude PII from event payloads and align tracking with your legal team's data policies.

Can you run campaigns for crypto or Web3 companies?

Yes, with the right setup. Google requires crypto advertiser certification; Meta has strict asset restrictions for crypto. We navigate these requirements and focus on compliant performance channels — often including programmatic DSPs and CTV where restrictions are lighter.

How long until we see results from fintech paid media?

Consumer fintech (neobanks, lending apps) typically sees CAC stabilization within 60–90 days as algorithms optimize on account opens or funded accounts. B2B fintech ABM programs take 90–120 days to build pipeline, with SQL velocity improving measurably by month 3.
