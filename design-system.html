FAQ — .faq-grid with <details>

Vora uses a results-first model: we report on ROAS, CAC, and LTV — never impressions or vanity reach. Every decision is grounded in data from $100M+ in managed spend.

What is a typical ROAS improvement timeline?
Most clients see 20–35% ROAS improvement in the first 30 days as campaigns gather data. Month two typically delivers 40%+ gains as AI bidding models mature.
Do you work with brands under $50K/mo in ad spend?
Our minimum engagement is $25K/mo in ad spend. Below this threshold, the data volume required for our AI models to outperform manual bidding is insufficient.
How do you measure CAC and LTV?
We implement full-funnel attribution via GA4 + first-party data pipelines. CAC is tracked per channel and campaign, LTV is modelled from 12-month cohort data.
What makes Vora different from other NYC agencies?
We've managed $100M+ in ad spend across finance, DTC, SaaS, and hospitality. Our AI creative testing infrastructure runs 200+ ad variations simultaneously — no other agency in New York operates at this scale.
